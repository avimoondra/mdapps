Summary of Application Experience

10/03/07
I've applied to a ton of schools and am waiting to hear back from most. I've got 3 interviews so far, and am supposed to hear back from at least 2 more this week.

10/09/07
Still waiting to hear back from Mayo (they claim they should get back to me in the next day or two). Rejection from Wisconsin came in the mail today.

10/21/07
Got a few rejections, and mayo didn't pan out. Had three interviews, and I think they all went pretty well. Will update when I hear anything.

10/24/07
Called Wayne at 1:05 this afternoon, I GOT IN! I can't believe it, after four years of such hard work, it has finally payed off!!!

11/1/07
I checked my status page, and BAM! I got into Cincinnati! I loved that school, and am REALLY excited about this one!

11/28/07
Haven't heard anything positive in a month, oh well. I withdrew from Wayne today, so if nothing else comes through, it looks like I'll be in Cincinnati in August!

12/21/07
Waitlisted (kinda) at Albany, and rejected a few places. I was offered an interview at EVMS, but would not go there over Cincinnati, so I declined. I wanna hear from some of the top schools, one way or another!

1/14/08
I'm still waiting to hear from so many schools! Would they just reject me already? Rejected at Iowa, UNC and Cornell recently, yuck.

1/19/08
I got 5 rejections this week. Wow, do I hate myself.

1/23/08
Man, has it been a rough week. I think I've found out about 7 or 8 rejections(including a couple unofficial as well).

3/25/08
Well, the last of the rejections came yesterday (Stanford), which means I'm 100% going to Cincinnati next year! I truly appreciate all of the schools that were willing to look past a few years of bumps and scrapes and give me a shot. I hope my success encourages others to chase their dreams, even if it seems hopeless... its not.
