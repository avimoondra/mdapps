Will be Michigan resident before the school year starts, filed application for Michigan State right on the deadline and called Wayne State to see about re-opening my file in light of new residency consideration; Phone Conference scheduled with Dean on 11/29!

11/14/2005- Received a call that my grandfathers condition is worsening so planning a trip home this weekend, will have to postpone arranging 2nd component (Community Physician Interview) of U of AZ Interview

11/4/2005- Well I was going to head to MD to be with my family & my grandfather, but my dad (a general surgeon) managed to get him released AMA and drove him up to his home in upstate NY so he could spend his final days, months, whatever in the home he loves. Besides everytime I talk to my grandfather he just asks if I have any news, tells me to stay busy with what I am doing and to not waste my $$ on a trip to see him. Hopefully I'll get calls from the DC/MD schools in a couple of weeks so I can try to take some time to drive to NY to visit while I am on the East Coast. On another crappier note, I got my first rejection today! What a downer!

'I regret to inform you that we cannot further consider your application for the entering Freshman Class of 2006. Wayne State University School of Medicine..... Our average GPA and MCAT for the 2005 entering class was 3.51 & 9.8 (29)'

Oh well, it is what it is, I wonder what I could have done aside from performing better on the MCAT that would have made a difference in my application!

On a brighter note, I did accept a job as a medical documentation editor and transcriptionist so I can stay up on medical terminology and familiarize myself with dictations, SOAP notes, and what to look for in an office visit. It is a very flexible gig, won't interfere with my volunteer work, I get to work from home so I get to stay with my little girl, and I can supplement our family income and hopefully cover all the costs of the whole Med School application process.

Oh and by the way, damn, suit shopping is such a bear for women! I am glad I finally found one I liked, just in time for my interview Tuesday!
