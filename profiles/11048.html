Summary of Application Experience

hey if you have any questions or think that i may be able to help you, email me at [email protected]

well, here it goes...

--8/7 all of my letters are now in. just a matter of time before i'm complete at my first school!

--8/17 i checked my status at ohio state, and they gave me an interview. sweet.

--9/2 Harvard gave me an interview! I am honored. Other updates: UPitt was amazing, and Case really impressed me as well. That new curriculum of theirs was as impressive as the rumors made them seem to be.

--10/XX Harvard was nice. The facilities, the amazingly lighthearted interviewers, the opportunities...So awesome that they even consider me for their school. The interview was so laid back and i showed so little of myself that im having a hard time justifying that they would actually pick me. ah well, the experience was cool.

UofM was cool as well. They were very...succinct. Ill know in a couple of days whether i got in or not...

--10/15 UofM accepted me! My god, im going to med school! Unbelievable. A dream comes true.

--10/15 Ah crap, placed on hold by Case. and i thought that interview day went well! Just goes to show you how random this process can be, i guess. That's ok, Umich has me on a high right now.

--10/17 UPitt acceptance! When i went to pitt i was so impressed by everything: the location, the students, the facilities (esp WISER). UPitt was THE standard i held every other school up to thus far, and it's awesome that i actually now get to make a tough decision between umich and upitt.

--10/25 Wayne State acceptance!

--11/24 Um WOW!!!!! uchicago gave me a nice scholarship. Can't wait for second look weekend...


--11/xx WashU was amazing, actually. before i visited i was a little concerned about their reasons for 'only' accepting kids with high mcats, but after going i understand their philosophy much better. everything was new, the students and people there are VERY friendly, and the buildings were gorgeous! I guess we'll see, but washu exceeded my expectations.

--12/21 So Hopkins gave me the call this morning! Why me?! why NOT me, i had been telling myself (and thinking up a hundred reasons why not). I dont even know if i deserve it...but this is awesome! a great christmas present!!!

--3/20: Rejected from Hahvahd, waitlist at Washu. Bummer. Not really surprised by the harvard decision, since i checked on their finaid page a week ago and got the 'bad message'.

Washu apparently has a two-tiered waitlist system? my letter said that 'i would thrive at washu, they just dont have the spot for me, but they take 50 people off the waitlist each year and if i wished to pursue the waitlist, i should send in these forms'. i actually have no idea if this was the higher waitlist, come to think of it. but ill send in the forms and see what happens.

I'm still leaning towards JHU, but Uchicago and Umich are very good options that i just cant ignore. it's hard to take them seriously, though, when ive got this acceptance to hopkins in the palm of my hands....

--3/30: So these past couple of weeks have sucked. Lots of rejections and waitlists, etc etc. But today Michigan offered to give me 100k, 25k per year in scholarship money. yeea boi! I'm so confused, i have no idea where i should go. On the one hand, Michigan is in-state for me and is an amazing school (plus scholarship). On the other hand, Hopkins has been a dream of mine to go to since high school. and I really feel like life is calling me to get out of my comfort zone, get out of Michigan and take a chance. I REALLY hope Hopkins need-based scholarship is good. I just dont know, but i understand that this is a great situation to be in. Ill keep this updated with where i stand, especially as soon as those financial aid packages come in...

--5/17: I decided to attend Hopkins. Yay!
