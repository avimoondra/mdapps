sent update about SPRI
Interview went great very conversational. The interview day was very informative but slightly long in terms of presentations. Campus, school and opportunities available were all great.

My two sense for future applicants.
Absolutely great school and great atmosphere. Personally, I was not a huge fan of the city, and the research opportunities and study abroad options made it less attractive to me. That being said still a great place to obtain your MD. I believe a key to being accepted post-interview at this school is updates and LOIs. They specifically sent you an email post interview asking you to update them on everything. Basically saying please tell us what you are doing so we know you are still interested. I think a failure to do so lands you on the waitlist. I could be off but I know both me a good friend who had previous acceptances when applying here and thus did not send updates/LOIs and thus were waitlisted. Know tohers who were on the ball with updates and LOIs who were accepted. Of course could just be because I was less qualified or performed less adequately on the interviews but I honestly believe they put a lot of stock in the students interest in the school.
