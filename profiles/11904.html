Summary of Application Experience

I included some secondary application costs that I kept track of as well as some dates. I am glad that I applied to so many schools because I would never have thought of a school like EVMS or Penn State if I only applied to 6-10 schools. I am dissapointed about UCONN, my state school. The only school I am anxiously waiting to hear from is Stony Brook. I know everyone says apply early, but it is very true. I took the MCAT in aug. 2001 and got a 26! I retook Aug 2003 after a prep course and got a 31. If you can I recommend taking the MCAT the Aug before your application year because you can retake in april if you want. As of now I am headed to Penn State unless EVMS or NYMC offer me a better financial aid package. Even though penn state has high tuition, the student budget was slightly less than drexel and others due to low cost of living. I will also go to Stony Brook if accepted off the waitlist.
