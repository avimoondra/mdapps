Summary of Application Experience

6/23/05 - Submitted AMCAS

6/29/05 - AMCAS Processed w/messed up GPA; sent grade change request

7/08/05 - AMCAS grades fixed; first secondary received

9/01/05 - I've received all the secondaries except pitt (hello?)

9/12/05 - first interview - UMASS (woohoo!) - will be on 9/27

11/03/05 - 7 interviews (2 completed, and I had a great time at both) and 2 rejections.

I am really anxious to hear back from UMASS or Tufts because an acceptance will allow me to withdraw my application from some schools - BU, UCONN, and GW for sure.

12/16/05 - I loved Minneapolis!

2/2 - was waitlisted at Sinai, and that sucks. I have told them that they are my first choice and I will withdraw other applications if they accept me. And now, that includes a scholarship at the University of Minnesota (phone call from Dr. Becker this afternoon!) I'm really happy about it and UofM is an AWESOME school, definitely one of my top choices. I would be happy there.

3/19 - only school I have yet to hear anything from is Yale. This is discouraging, because at the beginning of this process I knew it was one of my top 3 choices (with Sinai and Cornell as the other two). So, I wrote them a really personal letter all about how I identify with their philosophy: they are kind of like the 'Brown' of medical schools and I chose to apply to Brown (which was my first choice undergrad) because that was the kind of academic environment I craved, and still do. I don't know, it just felt sort of special to me. I know I fall short of their stats, but I still expected some sort of acknowledgement...

5/18 - phone call from UMASS! Tough decision but I chose UMass

7/13 - E-mail - I'm in at Tufts! ::conflicted::

7/18/ - Tufts it is!

OH MY GOD THIS IS A LONG PROCESS!!
