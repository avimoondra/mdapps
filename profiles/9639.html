Summary of Application Experience


Well, not so bad. Though, I think that applying to medical school might be more consuming than any other type of graduate school application process. And, I would say that if someone were unsure of whether they wanted to go to medical school it wouldn't hurt at all to take a few years off and gain some life experience. In fact, it might be to your advantage if you do decide to apply later; schools like life experience. That's about it. I have been pretty fortunate in the whole process and it has treated me well.
