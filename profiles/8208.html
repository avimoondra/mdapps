I was kind of disappointed since I usually do well on the verbal section, but ran out of time during the sept exam!! oh well

The other double degrees are in Bioethics... my major is Philosophy so I have a good base for that.

I know I am applying to some pretty selective schools, but you never know what can happen i guess. and i think im applying a little late, but financial contraints would not allow me to apply any earlier (unfortunately), so hopefully something will work out.
