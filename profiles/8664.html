8/10: Thanks for the personal message, as it has stimulated an update. I have 14/19 secondaries submitted as of this date, and my committee letter is being uploaded onto VirtualEval as I type. I am hoping that late next week I will get confirmations of completed applications, and then the waiting period for interviews will begin. Note: SUNY Buffalo and SUNY Upstate do not participate in VirtualEval, so you will have to send paper copies of your letter/letters. Good luck to you all!

8/20: VirtualEvals sent me an email notifying me that my letters are uploaded and available to all the participating schools. I now am waiting for emails to verify my completed application. I have only Georgetown and Stoneybrooke secondaries left to finish.

8/28: I am complete at all 18 schools I sent secondaries into (i could not justify Georgetown and could not see myself at Stony Brook).

9/17: First INTERVIEW!!!!!!!!! Tufts for MD/MPH Heyo!!!!! Interview there November 1st

11/6: Had a positive experience at Tufts last Thursday. Very impressed with the students, administration and facilities. I believe I interviewed well. We shall see in 5-6 weeks. In the time since my last posting, however, I have been rejected by both Dartmouth and Tulane. Ah well.

11/22: Pitt rejection. I guess I wont be heading back to my alma mater

12/10: Got put on 'Active consideration' at Tufts last week. I will send them another letter of interest/update letter and letters of recommendations from my gap year activities. Also decided to apply to some osteopathic schools sorta late. GW rejected me.

12/12: SUNY Downstate rejection. This was a bit surprising as I was accepted and waiting for a spot last year. Fortunately, the possibility that I may still get into Tufts has lessened this blow.

2/4: In the past two months I have inundated the Tufts adcom with letters of interest, additional letters of recommendation, and the holier than thou letter of intent. Also applied to several DO schools. Got interview invite from NYCOM today

4/10 Accepted at NYCOM. This is cool as it boasts probably the best clinical education of any DO school and I can attend with my twin brother. Probably moving from 'active consideration' at Tufts to the wait list. If Tufts wants me, they best tell me by June or I am heading to New York.

7/25 Got into Tufts today. Have an apartment, loans, and twin brother at NYCOM. Have to decide by 8/1 where I will go. Fuck.

7/28 On my 24th birthday, I have decided to attend Tufts. Im a Jumbo.
