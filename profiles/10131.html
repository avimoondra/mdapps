Summary of Application Experience

I applied very late in the process (AMCAS verified 11/15), due to misinformation. I had no guidance in the process until October, when I hired a Kaplan admissions consultant as there is no real premedical advisor or premedical committee at my tiny liberal arts school (~400 students).

1/5: Woot! UVM interview invite! :)

1/23: Interviewed at UVM...incredibly excited about the facilities, curriculum, everything. Fingers crossed!

1/26: Two rejections in one day stings.

1/31: BU Rejection...

2/8: Got a few more rejections, and a 'Hold' from GWU, but my UVM status page updated to say they had made a decision but weren't telling me yet...I'm so nervous I could...be really nervous...


2/11: UVM Waitlist...

2/21: GWU official rejection, still no word from UVM

2/24: EMT-B exam today!

3/1: Dartmouth Rejection.

3/14: Two rejections: Tufts and OHSU. Already knew about Tufts. But I did pass my EMT-B Practical!

4/6: Albany Interview invitation, for 4/13 (Friday the 13th...)

4/19: I thought the interview went really well, and I guess it did! Six days later I got the acceptance! :) :) :)
