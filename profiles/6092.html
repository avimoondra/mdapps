Summary of Application Experience

Last semester i applied to 6 DO and 2 md. Withdrew all but one app b/c of financial problems. Got interview at NSUCOM and was alternate listed.
Major mistakes form last year: Had D orgo 2 that wasn't retaken (Got B- now), applied late (complete in november), didn't really know the application process too well, didn't have letters ready on time, doubted myself and didn't apply broadly. I feel I have corrected the major mistakes in my application and hopefully now I will be able to get in somewhere and make the dream a reality. Good luck to everyone else.

I might have to go there just so my gf can get her graduate degree as well and not have to split up. We'll see.
