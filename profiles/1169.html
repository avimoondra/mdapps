2/22/14 -- ACCEPTED! This came as a big surprise, since I interviewed way back in October and hadn't heard anything for awhile I assumed I had been waitlisted.

I loved interviewing here! It was nice of them to provide free housing in Olin for the night, and the pizza party was a great way to get to know the school. People sometimes complain about St. Louis, but Wash U is in the best party of the city and Forest Park is great. The interview itself was relaxed, my interviewer just wanted to get to know me and my application so he could present it to the committee. I liked how they did the tour -- instead of everyone going in one large group, students just took two people through the facilities, which made it easier to ask questions and learn about the school.
