Summary of Application Experience

This will be the first time applying (Fall 2007) to medical school, but this has been a life-long dream that was put on hold for many years. I will begin the application process shortly when AMCAS is opened for the 2007 application year.

I decided to apply to 20 schools, with a mixture of in-state schools, out-of-state schools in my MCAT range (I'll never be in range of any school with my cumulative GPA), and balance it with a few reach schools and a couple of extreme-reach schools (Yale, UNC-CH, UCSF...hey why not?)

Update: I had to withdraw my application from 4 schools after realizing that I didn't satisfy some of their special prereq requirements that most schools don't require, or that the school insisted on me finishing my second-degree or were upset that I left a PhD program.
