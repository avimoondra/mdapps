Summary of Application Experience

April 22: Admitted to UCSF PRIME-US! I have it down to UCLA and UCSF. I really like both schools.

April: I withdrew from USC.

April: I withdrew from everywhere except UCSF, UCLA an USC

April 7: alternate list at UCI post interview, withdrew

3/25: Withdrew from UC Davis post-interview.

3/21: UCSF!!!!!!!! Acceptance, i can not believe this. This has been such an emotional and exciting process...i am just so glad to be accepted to ANY medical school...and to have options is such a gift. CRAZYYYYY!

3/20: Withdrew from Iowa and Washington after UCLA/USC/UCSD acceptances....also withdrew from Drew and Michigan (post-interview)

OMG! 3 in one day!!!! UCLA thank you!!!!!!!

3/19: Two in one day! USC acceptance! UCSD acceptance!!!!! just speechless...and incredibly excited. I really love San Diego...

I feel so fortunate to have decision to make...a year ago I was afraid i would get no acceptances!

3/17:
Yale waitlist arrived: I was bummed about this. I was really surprised to have interviewed there, and loved the school. At least its not a rejection yet-- we'll see!

Also: UCLA sent my letter today (finally), yikes!

3/15: Finally waitlisted at U of Michigan, upper tier. Will most likely be withdrawing next week.

received stanford rejection last week.


3/9: rejection letter from Columbia. I expected one of these, but it would have been nice to visit.

3/5: letter arrived from NYU- rejection...

2/20: Rejected by a very sweet email from Boston U. I hope this is not the beginning of a long stream of rejections!

2/19: Accepted to MSU!! Although, I am declining the admission offer.

2/8: withdrew from GW: i had not heard anything in over three months, and won't choose them over U Washington...

2/5: UCSD interview invite! interviewed late february


February: I was waitlisted for an interview at UCLA-Drew. Probably going to withdraw from there at this point...

February: UCSF was amazing, as expected. They are supposed to let me know at the end of march...i think i might go crazy!!

1/22: withdrew from U of Chicago interview, one of the hardest decisions so far, b/c i think its a great school. i hope it was the right decision...
also withdrew from UIC interview...

I feel so lucky to have gotten a great amount of interviews, I hope I find the right fit for me. Right now, im really interested in Yale, UCLA, UCSF and U of Washington. This should be interesting!

1/19: GOT INTO WASHINGTON!!! Awesome school--now I dont want to fly to CHicago...might cancel the interview.

1/18/08: withdrew from Einstein interview. I actually really wanted to go, but dwindling funds made it difficult.

1/08: UC Davis interview was great! I really was pleasantly surprised by their program. U Washington was also amazing, great school.

1/14: Mt Sinai interview invite, probably the most interesting NY med school to me

1/11/08: w/drew from Jefferson and Rosalind Franklin, NYMC, Kansas

1/10/08: Declined Drexel's interview invitation, it was hard to do, but wanted to free up the space!

1/4: I got an email with a UCSF invite!!! I cant believe it! By far my favorite and top choice school! I NEVER expected to get one here!!!! Interviewing in Early February! (2/0*/08)

1/3: Withdrew post secondary from Albany today. I would not choose them over Iowa, so no point in making them consider me.

1/2: Medical College of Wisconsin Interview invitation

12/28: Declined U of Wisconsin Interview: no money to fly, and liked Iowa!

12/21: U of Washington Interview Invitation! (Jan) they scheduled around my UC Davis interview so I am going from LAX --> Sacramento --> Seattle --> home!

U of Wisconsin Madison invitation! (1/11)
