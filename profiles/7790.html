Summary of Application Experience

9/09: Still in the middle of secondaries hoping to turn in Gtown, UChicago, Rush and Rosalind Franklin by end of week. So far have interviews at GW in October and UIC in 2 weeks.

10/09: Decided to forgo submitting Rosalind Franklin, but I could change my mind depending on if hear back from UIC. Have interviews at Temple 11/01, UVa 11/05, Rush 11/07 and Loyola 11/09 (3 states, 4 interviews in 8 days)So far I have submitted 11 secondaries and I have been extended 6 interviews and no flatout rejections which I am very happy about(though my app on hold at UC is not promising and Northwestern has gone MIA). Just waiting to hear from UIC soon

I feel that I am taking a minor risk by only submitting 11 secondaries but the schools I cut out I couldnt bring myself to work on their apps which told me that I probably didnt want to go there and should put more focus on the ones I care about. I'll find out in a couple weeks (from UIC) if this was a wise decision.

10/19: Interviews at GW and absolutely loved it. Wont hear from them for 6- 8 weeks though.

10/25: Got accepted to UIC! Huge weight off my shoulders. I'm off Temple and UVA for my next interviews this week.

11/09:
Just finished the most stressful week ever. Three interviews this week. Really really loved Rush and kind of really liked Loyola. Got waitlisted at GW which I was kind of bummed about but everything I liked at GW I loved at Rush. I hope all goes well with them in the near future.

11/17:

Got a letter from UVA and an email from Temple. Accepted and accepted. Very happy and excited! Not sure how I feel about Temple. I really didnt like Philadelphia and I'm not sure I would go there over a Chicago school or my Alma Mater. Also go my first rejection letter from Georgetown but I'm not in the least bit heartbroken. Crossing my fingers to hear favorably from Rush and Loyola within the next couple of weeks!


12/23

Received a phone call and an email from both of my interviewers at Loyola telling me that I had been accepted. Made me feel really special, Loyola is very good at being personal with their applicants.
NOTHING yet from Rush, they said I'm in committee though its getting really late and I'm starting to freak out.


12/29

Got into Rush!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
I am one lucky son of a bitch.


1/25
Extended an interview to U of Chicago after a long wait.


1/15/08 So its been almost exactly a year since I updated this so I will just give the run down: Near the end of this process it really came down to money. Loyola gave me nothing, UIC gave me everything and Rush got me halfway. HOWEVER after a 2nd visit to UIC and Rush, I could not see myself at UIC AT ALL no matter how much money they extended to me (this is not a bash on UIC it just wasnt a good fit for what I was looking for). I chose Rush and to my great surprise, I got extra money from them right before the deadline so the money difference was justifiable. I am so so happy I chose Rush. M1 year is going smoothly and I wouldnt change my decision for the world.
Word of advice to applicants: Hold your acceptances for a while because it can become a great financial bargaining chip as deadline approaches. But at the same time, if you absolutely would never go to a school, free up that spot for someone else within a reasonable timeframe.
