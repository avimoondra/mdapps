Summary of Application Experience

I took the MCAT in August 2006, the last paper and pencil, and I got a 27. I had a pretty rough summer and I didn't commit to studying. I retook the MCAT on July 13, 2007 and got a 33.

8/01/07 Submitted Amcas

8/23/07 Amcas verified.

8/26/07 Back from vacation. Tons of secondaries to fill out.

9/6/07 Pre-medical committee evaluation

9/10/07 Went back to my second job at a school. Definitely have less time for secondaries now.

9/11/07 Pitt interview invite!!! I was shocked because I did not complete the secondary yet. I guess I should get started.

9/21/07 LORs arriving at schools

9/2?/07 Complete at Penn

9/24/07 Decided that I needed to leave my second job for a while until I get my applications in because I definitely don't have enough free time and its getting late in the season.

10/2/07 Finally complete at Pitt

10/21/07 Submitted Drexel secondary

10/24/07 Had to fax my Jefferson application over because it wont let me submit it online (after numerous attempts). This is probably going to hold my application up even longer

11/7/07 Interview at Pitt!! I fell in love with the school. The WISER Institute was amazing, it had 23 simulators, even a mother that gives birth. I really felt at home in Pittsburgh.

11/15/07 Submitted Yale secondary

11/26/07 Complete at Emory.

12/01/07 Interview at Drexel. I live in Philly so I thought it was be nice to go to a Philly school. I liked the fact that the school has 2 different curricula so suit different learning styles. The technology was amazing. All the lectures are online for the IFM curriculum. The school kind of reminded me of high school for some reason (maybe the cafeteria and the lockers in the halls). A lot of the students said that they only went to Drexel because they didnt get into anywhere else (although I'm sure there are students that actually choose the school). I dont feel like it is the school for me.

12/5/07 Submitted Temple secondary. Complete at Howard

12/7/07 Accepted to Pitt!!!! I had a really good feeling about this school. It is definitely one of my top choices. I haven't had an interview at Penn yet and i'm not on hold so I don't want to count them out just yet. Yale still hasn't updated my status online. Complete at CASE. They were a late addition.

12/10/07 Penn interview invite! I'm so excited. I would love to go to this school

12/11/07 I have gotten a lot of good news in the last week. I randomly checked my Temple status and I had been invited for an interview. No idea when the invite was given. they should really send an email or something.

Received a status change email on 12/6 for Case. When I looked I thought it was just telling me I was complete. I curiously checked it after the Temple invite and low and behold an invite dated 12/6. Wow!!!

12/12/07 I'm debating on whether or not I am going to my interview at Case. I have to make a list of pros and cons or something. The plane tickets are like $300 but the greyhound tics are about 100 but it will take like 10-11 hours to get there.

12/13/07 Interview invite from Jefferson

12/17/07 Finally complete at Yale

12/20/07 Interview invite from Yale!! I was shocked and pleasantly surprised.

1/11/08 Penn Interview. I love this school. It has everything I want and more. I don't know how well my interviews went because my grandmom died the week before (at u of penn hospital) so I know I was definitely distracted. It really hurts because this school is so amazing and I was worried about canceling my interview because I tried to change the date previously and they said it would be near impossible because it was so near the end of the interview season. I just hope my grandmom is watching over me and helps me to make my dreams come true. I love you grandmom.

1/16/08 Didnt go to Temple interview. I was still kind of stressed over the Penn interview and my grandmom. I didnt feel as bad when I found out they are basically interviewing for the waitlist.

Also got an Howard interview invite. I'm going to withdrawal because I wouldnt go there over Pitt and I changed my mind about D.C.

1/18/08 Withdrew from Case and Howard.

1/23/08 I was really impressed. After interviewing at Penn I didn't expect to like the other Philly as much as I like Jeff. I believe Jeff has its own strong points esp if your not really interested in research. Dr. Christian and I had a wonderful conversation, it didn't even feel like an interview. I'm going to have a tough decision to make in May. Its definitely going to come down to second looks.
