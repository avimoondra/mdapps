Summary of Application Experience

Everything I need to know I learned in Hell (aka this application process) LOR version:
DON'T BE SHY. I called and emailed a handful, made no progress, and almost gave up on applying to schools that require a DO letter, which would have sucked because I love DO. What I found: Let EVERYONE you know know that you are applying and trying to find a physician to shadow/talk to--ultimately with the need of a letter. Turns out a woman in my mother's mah jong class at the senior center is a retired drug rep with connections. AND my aunt set me up with a Mayo Clinic anesthesiologist. AND my aunt's friend is working on setting me up with a third. Awesome. :)
