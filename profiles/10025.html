Summary of Application Experience

5/14/07 Finally withdrew from Buffalo. I am now hoping to get off of UCLA's waitlist. What a difference a year makes. Who would have thought I would be so close to staying in Cali for Med School?

3/23/07 My interview at UCLA went very well. I hope they let me know one way or the other if I am in or not soon. I have to make my way to Nashville to check out the house I MIGHT be renting come June.

3/15/07 I can not believe I have an interview at UCLA!!! I wanted to get out of Cali....but saving 20k a year and going to a top of the line med school is an opportunity I could not turn down!

3/7/2007 Since UCLA decided to let me send them 60 bucks for a 2ndry I said WTH and turned it in. UCI sent me a 2ndry award too...but I would never go there. So now I wll NEVER DO ANOTHER MEDSCHOOL 2NDRY IN MY LIFETIME!!!

2/5 Just got back from London and Paris!!! I have cancelled interviews at Upstate and Howard. I am now getting prepared mentally for my move from SUNNY CALI to NASHVILLE, TN! I have to find an off campus apartment because I have a puppy that I just can't leave behind. The only way I won't be going to Nashville (short of divine intervention) is if I get an interview and acceptance from EMORY.

12/11 Two for Two! I am done with this process! I am going to take a vacation with the money I was going to use interviewing at other schools. I absolutely loved Meharry!!! I know I will be happy there for the next four years!

11/10 I feel so blessed that I have an acceptance relatively early in this process. I am going to go on a few more interviews but BUFFALO is a great school so I know no matter what I will be happy.

10/22 First interview request. I am hoping the first of many. I am getting no love from my home state. I think I will make some calls about the secondaries. I have slowed down sending in secondaries...I am running out of money. Emory is my top choice. I will turn in Keck's but I am having trouble with the essays.

9/5/2006 Did the easy secondaries first. I hate writing essays!!!

8/24/20006 AAMCAS verified. Not happy with this UG-GPA but what can I do. At least I did better with my grad work.

8/23/2006 I applied to too many schools! I freaked out at the last minute and decided to apply to a lot of schools to increase my chances (if that makes sense). I'm waiting on one LOR from a liberal arts teacher before I can submit some of my secondaries.

Submitted AAMCAS 07/28 and it's still 'ready for review' ARRRRGGG!!!
