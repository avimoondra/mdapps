Note: So you found my status eh? I strongly encourage you to start your own profile. Not only does it help you keep track of your progress, it helps fellow premeds like you and me. We're in this business to help people right? Premeds are people, too!

Especially you, UC Berk students, start making profiles!


5/30/06: to take my mind off of mcat scores which should be out in about 2 weeks, I decided to start on my personal statement. I also wrote to my English grad student to see if she would be able to write me a strong supportive letter for med school. I'm keeping my fingers crossed

5/12/06: doing my best to avoid going to online premed forums, they're seriously a waste of time. I won't start applying till in June.

6/20/06: Just received my scores. I'm one point short of my goal but I was still pleasantly surprised when I saw the numbers. If i could do anything differently, I would have done less full-length exams during the week leading to the exam (i did 3 total in that week alone), and instead I woulda practiced more verbal passages.

I'm leaving to go study abroad in Madrid, Spain tomorrow. I'm a little worried about doing primaries out of the country but there's no way I'm letting anything get in the way of a once in a lifetime opportunity.

7/14/06: Wow, working on primaries with absolutely minimal internet connection was a huge PAIN. I'm still having a wonderful time studying abroad. I strongly recommend it to you premeds! Before getting immersed into the world of medicine, immerse yourself into the sea of humanity by studying abroad in a foreign country. It has been frustrating at times, but the culture is beautiful and the people I've met have been delightful. I definitely have a deeper appreciation for the cultural differences of others.

I just turned in my primaries thanks to my friends back at home and my new found friends over here =)

7/30/06: Got back from a weekend excursion to Sevilla, a lovely town with very unique architecture in the buildings and layout of the streets. It has a VERY diverse history with a mix of Muslim/Jewish/Christian/Catholic influence.

Anywho, I just received a secondary from NYMC even though I'm still waiting for my primary app to be verified by AMCAS. I'll be working on secondaries when I'm back in cali. after this program. It's highly recommended that you finish off secondaries asap but I'd rather enjoy my last three weeks in Spain, take a huge breather, and lose myself in this experience a little more before working on apps again.

8/2/06: AMCAS finally verified my primaries. My trip still is not over but i?m updating this for all of you premeds.

9/17/06: Came back with a crap load of secondaries with due dates that already passed. Called some schools to ask for extensions, been working on essays, working part-time in lab, I really really want to go back to ER volunteering but my schedule's booked =(, I really should have worked on secondaries earlier but oh well. I have a SUPER supportive GF who has been my encouragement and my rock of faith. Thank you God for her!

10/9/06: Wow, only three 2ndaries sent in. Been real busy with life. Hopefully I can finish up the rest soon. My last LOR finally came in last week. I'm set... cept I have to do more essays...

10/12/06: yes! my first comment! nothing like inspiration to keep updating, kinda like eprops on xanga. anywho, i just sent in stanford and UCI (i know, i'm pretty late in the game). You probably noticed a pattern. I'm completing all Cali apps first and then hopefully I can zoom through the OOS schools.

I might as well give a few pointers to you guys out there. The UC's usually have a 30 day deadline for their secondary requests and because I came back from Spain a bit late in August, a few deadlines have passed or were about to pass. Needless to say, I freaked out and called UCI, UCSD, and UCD to ask for extensions. UCD ppl were super nice and extended it for 3 weeks. UCI was very laid back and said just turn it in before dec. The person at UCSD didn't seem too thrilled by my request (and with good reason, of course) but she did extend it for another week. I also called Wakeforest which has a 2 week deadline and they're as laid back as UCI. They said before Dec. =)

I also went to NY, NY from 9/21 to 9/27 so I'm very thrilled about the possibility of going to a NY school. I'm open to new experiences.

Oh yeah, I dropped Georgetown because of $$$ and because their secondary is too annoying to fill out for someone who doesn't really care about their school.

10/17/06: Just sent off C. Drew and let me tell you, what a secondary that was! I have the right background (lower working class and uninsured family while growing up/activities devoted to serving the underserved) but their questions were tough to answer. I suggest that you do not apply there if you doubt that you have a heart for the underserved, especially if you don't know what that service entails. It's not just another 'UCLA' folks. Don't waste your time if that's the main reason why you're applying.

I withdrew Loma Linda because they're a bit too conservative and plus, they hold mandatory chapel meetings. I'm not against SDA as a religion but I don't think any chapel services should be mandatory.

10/19/06: October is racing by and I have 4 more secondaries to go. But, a good thing is that I don't have a social life to kill (it died a few weeks ago). My role as a BF has come to question by my friends, my gf, and myself. Also, I've gotten skinny (lost muscle mass that I spent my last two years of HS and my first two years in college working for) and now I think I'm starting to get pudgy. But I ain't complaining! No sacrifice is too great for a doctor!! Yeah, I hope you find this humourous, too ;)

10/20/06: Two down, two more to go!

10/23/06: No more secondaries! whoohoo! Time to go out and have a social life again.

Update: I just noticed that UCI's status page indicated that they didn't get my LOR's. I gave em a call and it turns out that they received em on the 10/11 but since I submitted on 10/13, they didn't combine it with my apps! That's unusual since schools generally advise you to send letters in ASAP even before submitting the secondary. Needless to say, I'm glad I decided to give them a call. This whole process, like billions have said before me, is a CRAPSHOOT!

10/25/06: So, it's almost the end of Oct. and I'm just getting completion notices. We'll see if the premed mantra of 'turning everything in EXTREMELY early is the key' really does work... cuz I'm turning my s*** in pretty late. Wish me luck!

11/1/06: My first interview is scheduled! and at an early date too! Yay for UCSD

11/6/06: Finally a NY school notifies me that I'm complete. Yeesh, I was beginning to think that I wasted 500 bucks by applying to them and having them run away with my money. On another note, I emailed Wake Forest to let them know I'll be in Winston-Salem with my gf to visit her family again for the new years (I've been visiting since the past two years). Hopefully that'll make me a lil more appealing to that school.

11/7/06: AECOM is complete... and to think that I lost faith in NY schools.

11/10/06: Last week I found out that NYMC never received my LOR's for some reason. So I sent them another packet and now I'm finally complete. It's a VERY good thing I called and checked.

11/15/06: Formally withdrew from Georgetown & Loma Linda via email. bye bye!

11/16/06: This week is all about highs and lows. The UCI interview request is definitely a high. It's crazy how they've already accepted students and my interview is almost 2 months from now, but yanno what? I'm just gonna relax, accept whatever happens, and not worry about the applications process anymore. You should do that too.

11/19/06: Ouch, first rejection from UCSF. I knew it was coming, especially since I didn't receive a secondary from them (they received my file on 8/8). I'm more disappointed than I am devastated but I'm very lucky to have 3 interviews coming up and I'll definitely be applying there again in the future. I love SF!

Note: Schools that send you rejections by mail don't update your status page. Why can't they just update the status page and save paper?

12/2/06: Just thought I'd update for the hell of it. The SD interview went well (just be yourself, of course) and hopefully I'll hear back by Dec. 11 (Please Please Please!!) I still haven't gotten any responses from NY schools other than being complete. I'm starting to think it was a waste of money to apply there (each secondary cost about $100) but we'll see.

I'm keeping myself busy with work and spending time with friends and my gf and going to the gym. If I have free time, I study up on Spanish =) I might as well make the most of my year off, even if I end up having to reapply again. I just have to trust in God and I need to stop being so anxious.

12/10/06: Wow, Stanford was totally unexpected and although I'm flattered and would go there out of all my choices, I doubt that there's much chance left since I'm interviewing so late. Doesn't hurt to try right? Wish me luck in March!

12/12/06: I guess I'll have to wait till Jan 9th to hear back from UCSD because one or both of my interviewers haven't turned in their report on me. It's all good. There's nothing like delayed gratification. This whole process has been about waiting and I'm pretty much used to it now.

1/9/07: Thank you God! I'm IN!!!!! UCSD was my first love and is now my first acceptance. I feel like a ton of weight on my shoulders just vanished. Now to enjoy the rest of my year off, work hard, and be devoted to my gf. As far as I'm concerned, I'm practically done! I hate hearing this (and you probably do too) but it's my turn now: I'm going to be a doctor!

*2/8/07: Advice time! Those of you wondering why I withdrew from Davis, of course it was because of acombination of a UCSD acceptance and a pre-interview hold (which came really late btw). However, if you are ever placed on a pre-interview hold and you are still interested in attending, let the schools know of your interest asap by sending them a letter or email (updates on what you're currently doing, intent, etc.) I've seen some people get off of holds lists that way so don't just sit around and wait.
