I am unbelievably relieved that this ordeal is over. On to the next one now! I can't wait for classes to start this fall!

5/7/07-Finally got my rejection letter from CCLCM. Screw that school for making me waste my money by flying to cleveland and interviewing with them when their class was already full.

5/15/07-Received another post-interview rejection letter from OHSU.
