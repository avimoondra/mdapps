Summary of Application Experience

In retrospect, I was in panic mode while doing the primary, I applied to waaaaaaaay too many schools and in the process wasted hundreds of dollars.

The same goes for secondaries, but in a much lesser degree. Still...hundreds in waste.

This is for all the withdraws. I pretty much stopped filling in secondaries after I got accepted to UTSW on 10/15. Oddly enough, I really did not like the culture of the school, it was very competitive and cutthroat feeling. Also, the campus was so gloomy. I definitely want to stay in california, but UC Irvine waitlisted me, Davis put me on 2nd tier waitlist and told me 'slim chance' over the phone, and UCLA waitlist really isnt moving this year. I really think the UC system is a failure.

However, I'm glad to be going to UTSW this fall even though I hated it so much in the beginning. I hated it so much that I wrote a scathing interview feedback page for it. However, I everyone that has been in contact with me from that school has been super nice. I have emailed the head honchos some quick questions and they respond right away and very nicely too. The tuition is freaking awesome even for OOS students, and I do have some friends and family in the area.
