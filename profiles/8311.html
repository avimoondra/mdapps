5/20/08 - All done!

12/1/07 - EEK! Baylor! Dean called and left a message while I was at work. I've been carrying my phone obsessively waiting for this! I didn't think they'd call on a Saturday so I left my phone on silent. Still so excited!

11/15/07 - Pittsburgh acceptance!!! via snail mail! I also sent out my last secondary today, it's a wonderful day :)

11/8/07 - Hurry up, Baylor! So anxious...

10/20/07 - Beautiful Baylor...

10/16/07 - AHHHHHHHHHH! So happy! Thank you Vandy! So excited when I got the call I think I almost yelled 'Hello' to the dean. He called at about 11pm while I was brushing my teeth so I had to oh so subtly spit toothpaste during our conversation. Too much information I know, but I thought it was funny. I'm so excited everything is funny!

10/10/07 - So happy with Pittsburgh! Even though I'm probably not going to do to well on my midterms and such since it was right in the middle of all that. Totally worth it. Hopefully Baylor goes well too.

9/25/07 - Yay for Baylor invite! My committee letter actually didn't get mailed until 9/19, hopefully I'll hear from more soon.

9/8/07 - Still sending in the last of the rolling schools' secondaries...anyone know how late is 'too late' for submitting secondary apps?

8/24/07 - Spoke to soon! Letters of rec went out today!

8/22/07 - TMDSAS sent out to schools. So many secondaries! My committee letter has yet to be sent out even though I interviewed in June :( I guess that means it's okay that I've been slow with the secondaries?? I'm not sure if most schools wait until you're 'complete' before they invite you to interview. Pitt and Vanderbilt didn't wait, I guess they're different. Do you have to have letters of rec in before you get invited to interview at most places?

8/1/07 - Finally submitted TMDSAS

7/05/07 - AMCAS sent out to schools. Got a lot of automatic secondaries within days of submitting. Thought I'd get a little break! :)

6/26/07 - Submitted AMCAS (I think I've applied to way too many schools!)
