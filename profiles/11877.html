Summary of Application Experience

12/22 - Well the application process is over for me. I'll be at UF next year!

11/2 - I changed my mind about Baylor too. I just don't feel like interviewing anymore. My mom made a good point, for me it is hard to come across as a genuinely interested applicant if I don't want to go to the school. I think some other schools have picked up on that (cough... USF - where I actually told another applicant that I didn't want to go there, oops! I just get nervous and chatty!).

10/31 - I don't feel like going to any more interviews. The more I hang out around UF's med school the more I want to go. I want to feel settled and get started already. I'm still planning on going to my Baylor interview at the end of this week since I already had the plane tickets. Don't think I will go to Wash U or any others.

10/15 - I heard from UF today :) Accepted!!! This is my first choice but I am still entertaining the idea of some other schools in case things change over the next year. My boyfriend goes to UF so this is a factor in me choosing this school.

10/4 - I didn't think that I'd get so many interview invites so quickly! I was kind of hoping they would be spread out so that I could get at least one acceptance and then I wouldn't have to go to all of the interviews.

9/22 - My first choice, UF just called me to ask me to interview! Schools have also been emailing to let me know my file is complete. Yay!

9/14 - My committee finally sent out my letters. Waiting for more interviews.

9/3 - Premed committee has STILL not written my letter. I can only imagine how much this hurricane coming for Florida is going to delay things.

8/15 - My premed committee is taking *forever* to write my letter. I submitted the request a month ago. I was upset but I tried to use my Vandy interview to get their butts moving. I guess I won't be getting anymore interviews until they finally send that letter out.
