Summary of Application Experience

Legend: Secondary (R) received date (S) sent date (C) complete date

After 3 weeks of prcessing, AMCAS sent me an email, stating to the effect, that their dog had ate my transcript!? So i immediately sent them 2 more on seperate days, just in case. submitted 7/20. processed 8/25. NOT impressed.

I have called some admin offices asking about my app and everyone was very nice and helpful.

Most of these secondary turn around times were 2-3 days or less. Usually 1 day. There are some discrepancies due to the fact that I knew that i could not complete no matter what until AMCAS verified my app. After 8/25 the secondaries were done pretty quickly in the order they arrived. Some were held over the weekend due to inability to mail during the weekend.

*NOTE: Your app cannot complete unless the school gets your verified AMCAS primary. This is here because I received secondaries from some schools even though AMCAS had not verified my application. I sent those in and could not complete until AMCAS verified my app about 1.25 months later.

I also applied last year but my LORs were not sent out until mid Nov. So I essentially completed in december. For those of you taking August MCAT and applying, if you cannot get everything in by late september, save your money and wait till next year.

I simply don't believe those schools that claim they are non-rolling. After all, when they start interviewing in September, do they really wait until january or later to make a decision to accept? I think not.
