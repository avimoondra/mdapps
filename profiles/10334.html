Summary of Application Experience

Well my glaring weakness is my EC's. I don't know about my LORs either, as I signed a consent form not to look at them. Also, my PS score is not good, not for what I should have been able to attain with my science gpa.


11/21/06- Got my first interview invite from OSU. I'm happy, lets me know my letters of rec must be acceptable. My EC's are still weak, imo. Anyway I'm happy with the invite, hopefully it won't be the only one, but it could be. I feel I did the best on my secondary questions from them and the rest of my secondaries did not go so well. Time will tell I guess. Also, my interview is on Feb. 14:( I feel that's gonna be a waitlist interview. Also, feeling nauseous and nervous is not going to be fun on valentine's day. I'm grateful for it though, don't get me wrong.

12/2/06- Finally got another interview invite. It's from Wayne State and I'm happy someone else liked me. Their curriculum looks somewhat interesting. I also got rejected from Pitt a few days ago and was depressed, because I loved the looks of that school. Oh well they're top 20 and I'm sure it doesn't help my stuff is somewhat late and I have a 9 in one of my subsections of the MCAT!

1/2/07- Don't know if my interview at Wayne went so hot. I was really hoping it would. I talked about abortion (ouch), my interviewer laughed at me( oh no), and I said I applied to 15 schools (which is true, those are how many secondaries I turned in) but my AMCAS says 18 and he didn't ask so he probably thinks I'm a liar.

1/15/07- After rejections from UChicago and URochester I was getting pretty desolate. I then got in a car accident last week. However, today I opened my email to find an NYMC invite. Yay! I love New York, and would love to be there for 4 years.

1/16/07- Another invite! Takes me to 4 interviews, now all I need is for one of them to turn into an acceptance. I'm sending updates to the rest of my schools tomorrow and am calling the 2 NY schools to mention I'll be in NY on Feb 28.

1/24/07- Received Albany invite, I could actually go Jan 31, but I need someone to drive with me to NY and my boyfriend can't take any more time off of work (I live in MI). I will think about what day to make it after my interview at MSU tomorrow.

1/29/07- I think the MSU interview went o.k. My student interview could have went better. I was nervous the first 15 mins and had to actually stop and restart my answer to one question. The faculty interview went better. Who knows? I feel my clinical experience is weak compared to others that they interview. I may find out tomorrow. I'm hoping to get accepted by them tomorrow or accepted by Wayne on the 31st. That will take the pressure off of my other 3 interviews. I finally sent my updates to the other schools. Let's hope that Feb leads to a few more invites for this cycle.

1/31/07- I got into Wayne! So excited, no matter what I am going to medical school next year.

2/10/07- Interview at Albany didn't go so well. I got a really tough interview that really tried to back me into corners. Oh well, we will see.
