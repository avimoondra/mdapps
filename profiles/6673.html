Summary of Application Experience

Second time around, wish me luck!

8/13/08: First interview invite! I'm really excited to visit Maine and interview at UNECOM!

8/20/08: Added AZCOM. Just feeling nervous since I haven't heard back from DMU, CCOM or Western.

8/21/08: Interview invite at VCU! Shut up! They must have liked my super nerdy essay. Scheduled for 10/2.

8/24/08: Got a letter today saying I'm rejected from EVMS. Not that damaged by it. Maybe if I didn't already have 2 interview invites :]

8/25/08: Interview invite at CCOM! I am waiting for interview dates to open up in October after my first two interviews are over.

8/26/08: Interview invite at Western! So excited; this is one of my top choices!!!

9/11/08: At the very end of the 30 days DMU takes to review files, they decided to invite me for an interview!

9/21/08: Rejection from U Wisconsin. I knew I had a small chance as an OOS applicant. At least that's one less file to worry about.

10/2/08: My first two interviews are done and I think they both went pretty well! I feel much better about the next few weeks and hope to hear some good news!

10/24/08: While traveling for interviews yesterday I found out I got into Western! 2 years of applications finally paid off! Im overjoyed but still waiting to hear from other schools before making any decisions.

11/10/08: Something I never thought would happen...UW interview! SO HAPPY!!!

1/29/09: The rejections slowly trickle in, one by one.

6/19/09: After a long wait, I have been accepted at VCU! Withdrawing from WesternU. I loved Richmond and the school was fantastic. Great staff and facilities.
