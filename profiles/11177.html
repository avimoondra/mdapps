I'm really not sure what it was about my application that the UC's hated so much. But I have complete faith the Lord will provide!

3/14/6- So, I was waitlisted at the U of MN yesterday. I shouldn't take it so hard since I have been accepted to two schools that I would be thrilled to go to if only I hadn't received an interview at the U of MN. Now my whole family thinks I should get in there, as well as all of my friends. I probably won't hear anything until May 15th, so here's to waiting!
