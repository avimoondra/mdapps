The waiting game sucks...

Second Acceptance at UMDNJ-SOM. Loved it here. 12/21

Third acceptance at UNECOM. 12/23 A lot of christmas presents for me. :P

***On a good spot on the USUHS waiting list...waiting with fingers crossed.

5/13
I'm in at the Military Med School, my top choice the whole time. :) I have to admit, I will miss learning OMT at the DO schools though. Final words, just don't give up. I got a lot of rejections, but held on to the waiting list at my top choice for several months and kept letting them know I was still interested. At the same time, I had a back-up plan at my second choice (where I was also accepted) at UMDNJ-SOM. Know what you want, and go for it with everything you got.
