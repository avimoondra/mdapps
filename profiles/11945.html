Summary of Application Experience

my amcas was submitted in june, all my secondaries were done by mid-september at the latest and my committee letter was sent out 10/4...

my advisor insisted I apply to at least twenty schools. So I did then found out about brown opening up their admissions to anyone and a friend was talking up McGill so I added them. I think I applied to too many, in retrospect, especially the california schools. I want to stay close to home.

3/3: let the record show that i am feeling VERY down in the dumps tonight after getting the columbia waitlist and no acceptance from duke (not sure if it will be w/l or rej). its so easy to get discouraged in this process, even if you have had a lot of great interview invites and some acceptances.

3/09: I got into harvard today, my absolute first choice!!! this is where I'll be going. this week is soooo much better than last week. now i don't care if the schools i'm waiting to hear from take me or not.

3/23: got put on a ton of waitlists and withdrew from them all for H. they make me feel even more lucky to have gotten into Harvard at all.
