Summary of Application Experience

Pretty much over this whole process. If I had it to do over, I would have cut my list down by about 5 schools, and all my drops would have all been safeties, especially state schools like UConn, OHSU, Maryland, and the stupid UCs. I got almost all my interviews with schools I regarded as 'pipe dreams', and wasted a bunch of money (I would conservatively estimate $6000 to date--sucks being a Southwesterner who has to fly everywhere when interview season rolls around) on schools that, evidently, didn't think I'd attend there. They were probably right. Waitlists didn't move the way I'd have hoped. Long process, tough to keep hopes up.
