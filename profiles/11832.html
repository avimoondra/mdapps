Summary of Application Experience

I am already accepted and deferred (open to reapply elsewhere) from the 2004 cycle, but I retook the MCAT and did a lot better, so I am trying again for better schools. Going through this process you learn a lot about what you are looking for from med school and conversely which schools are better suited to your goals.

Last year's profile: http://www.mdapplicants.com/viewprofile.php?myid=01152

Updates:
5/30/05: I can finally disclose that I will be attending the University of Alberta. This has definitely been a rollercoaster of a year, and a great learning experience! Good luck to everyone out there :)
3/24/05: Unbelievable, I got a Wash U full tuition scholarship (Olin)! I also got accepted by Stanford. I will be withdrawing from everywhere except the U of A and Wash U.
3/23/05: Holy smokes, I got a full tuition scholarship to Pitt! This totally revokes my first waitlist depression :)
3/22/05: waitlisted at Penn, which makes the decision easier. Withdrew.
3/2/05: Just found out about Pitt and Columbia!
2/25/05: I only have one interview left, at Stanford in a few days. Whew, it is nice to be near the end! I am also really excited to be a scholarship finalist at Wash U (found out today, will be back in STL in a few weeks for an extra interview).
2/3/05: I woke up this morning to a Stanford interview, which was shocking since I was sure my rejection letter had gotten lost in the mail. I also got a U. of Alberta interview, which is sweet since they rejected me w/o interview last year.
1/21/05: Since HMS is done sending invites, and I didn't get one, I am assuming that I have been rejected pre-interview. My first one, which is good. It keeps you humble! haha :)
1/20/05: Wash U acceptance!! Wow, I will be withdrawing from MSSM and BU now. :D
1/10/05: BU acceptance! So far things are going really well.
12/22/04: I heard from my interviewer that I got in at NYU! I also received a Wash U interview. This has been an amazing week/month for me.
12/21/04: Accepted to Cornell! I loved this school, so I am really overwhelmed. I will be withdrawing from my AECOM interview for sure.
12/5/04: Accepted to MSSM, on a Sunday!
11/22/04: Accepted to OHSU, in state so not a big surprise. Still nice though!
10/25/04: OK, I spoke too soon about AECOM. Got the invite today :)
10/22/04: Hmmm, nothing for three weeks. This waiting sucks--I want my life back. I can't figure out AECOM, since I was accepted there last year.
10/1/04: OHSU invite came today. I am going home with my babe for Halloween-fun!
9/28/04: I got Pitt today. Now I am going to start to plan another crazy interview trip for November. Getting things done in two trips would be a nice bonus, though.
9/24/04: I got MSSM and BU today. Wow!
9/22/04: I got a Columbia interview! I am going to have a crazy week in NYC.
9/16/04: NYU interview invitation, so at least I can get three interviews into one plane ticket. It would be great to add a school or two to that trip...
9/13/04: Cornell interview invite--wow!
9/8/04: UPenn interview invite--my first! I am really excited and my confidence is boosted a smidge again :)
8/20/04: I finally buckled down and added UPitt and Alberta. I have been thinking about this for a few months now.

8/13/04: I have completed my secondaries from all schools accept Harvard(I haven't heard from them yet). Now, I am just crossing my fingers for interviews...
