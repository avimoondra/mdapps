? ADVICE FOR FUTURE APPLICANTS ?
1. Apply early. Submit your AMCAS in June.
2. Get your LORs ASAP, Interfolio service was great for me.
3. Have fun on interview days! Relax, enjoy it, be yourself, be real.
4. If your interviewer gives you a card, or if information is provided, send a quick thank you note to them. Also, be friendly to EVERYONE you meet, admissions office, students, etc.
