Applying MD/PhD and MD only in Texas

You have to submit both AMCAS and TMDSAS. Next year, all MD/PhD programs will be through AMCAS, but you still have to 'check off' the MD/PhD box in TMDSAS.

BCM will only let you apply to MD/PhD or MD only. You cannot apply to both (whoops..).
The MD/PhD programs I was invited to: TCOM, A&M, UTHSCSA, UTMB, UTH.

TCOM - Extra interview during your interview day. Coordinated through the medical school only. DO/PhD decisions are made after the Match.
A&M - Additional day of interviews before your MD interview day. I was originally scheduled for MD only, but they rescheduled me once the MD/PhD committee invited me for an interview. The program coordinates with the medical school. Finally heard back in May... lol.
UTHSCSA - Invited for MD only and MD/PhD within a day of each other, with the MD/PhD interview the day after the MD only interview. Program coordinates with the medical school. I was also able to attend the yearly MD/PhD retreat, which was really fantastic. Decisions came out early January.
UTMB - Invited for MD/PhD only. No invite for MD only. The MD/PhD program interviews are in lieu of the MD only interviews. The program starts interviewing in October. Decisions came out in late January.
UT-H - Invited for both MD only and MD/PhD. MD was in September, MD/PhD in December. The MD/PhD program has four interview days, starting in October. Decisions came out in January.
