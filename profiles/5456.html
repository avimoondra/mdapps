Summary of Application Experience

Created final school list for my reapplication. At 51 schools, it's amazingly the same number as my first application and far more than I had intended. My MCAT expires during this cycle, though, so this is my last shot.

===//===

Rationale for some schools/states:

Connecticut, Oregon, Vermont, Virginia, Wisconsin - ties to area/school
Maryland - only school to interview me with previous application

===//===

6/3 - Chose VCU over Tulane and withdrew from all schools except Georgetown. SMP also ended yesterday!

5/23 - Had a change of heart about VCU, which took me off their waitlist on 5/19. I had ranked it below Tulane, but now I'm having second thoughts. Tough choice involving a lot of soul searching & daily flip flopping.

5/12 - Paid Tulane's deposit, so unless Wisconsin takes me off their list, I'm going to Tulane!

4/4 - Pretty sure the outcome of this cycle is decided. It'll be between Tulane and one other school, by the end, I think. I like Tulane, but the price tag and the instability five years after Katrina give me some pause. I'm also not sure what to make of their match list. If I don't get the luxury/curse of choosing schools, I'll still be thrilled with New Orleans and the next phase of my life.

12/17 - Happy with progress in SMP. Currently 3.75+ at the halfway mark
