Summary of Application Experience

*Looking forward to attending UC Davis!!! :)This has been the most grueling/stressful/exciting experience of my life, and I am so glad that it is over.

**To those applying: apply to a WIDE range of schools, and before you send in that check, think about whether or not you would actually go to that school if accepted (consider location, curriculum style, grading, etc). I ended up applying to many schools that I wouldn't have even attended if I got in. Also, be persistant and stay on top of your apps. Standing out amongst all the other great apps is a daunting feat, but you gotta give yourself all the ammo you can. Getting stuff in as soon as possible is also HUGE--as are early interviews. Be prepared for a serious emtional roller coaster. GOOD LUCK!!
