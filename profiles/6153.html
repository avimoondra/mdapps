-Interviews are fun but juggling them with school is a pain.

Quarter is almost over..
I have been very fortunate during the process so far.

I can't complain, I'm grateful for the outcome, though to be honest the process is inherently random (just sample the MDapp profile pool). Not that you can't sway your odds, good and thorough research into schools and interview preps always helps, but I'm not that disciplined of a person; so you can't be in the zone 100% of time for interviews.
