130123 Rejected (no invite email). I wrote CASPer on 121017 -- so glad it's over. Overall, not a great experience. Furthermore, the scenario videos were mostly useless parts of the exam, and certainly the fact that they didn't reuse questions for the scenarios they warned would reappear on the exam made preparing for them a waste of time.
