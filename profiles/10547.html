Summary of Application Experience

i re-took the August MCAT and didnt do as well as i should have done but we'll have to go with what i got and hope for the best while preparing for the worst because i got no one to blame but myself...life would be much easier if i woulda scored closer to my AAMC diags but its whatever...being asian doesnt help either but lets do this...call me the underdog because every dog has his day to shine...

10/31-interviews are starting to roll in...my father calls this time 'harvest time'...keepin my fingers crossed for some big names...

11/2- long awaited first interview at tech MBA/MD...did well but can do better...for the most part i was smooth...didnt have to really think too much...but some answers could have been more crisp...

11/4- houston was good....wasted bout 40 miles getting lost around the city....Dr. Gunn wasnt as boring as people had put him out to be...one interview went great and lasted less than 20 minutes...the guy basically asked me questions about my family..and how to fix healthcare...the 2nd interview was not as great...the lady downloaded the wrong application so i basically had to tell her my application....caused me to ramble on...lasted closer to 1 hr...i left pretty pissed..oh well...

11/8-school sucks...interviewing next wk at VCU and iowa...lookin forward to them though...first outta states...
also why arent texas schools showing me any love?

11/22-Back in Austin...had 2 interviews last week
VCU (11/16)-Very chill and the weather was nice much to my surprise...richmond seems pleasant and the people are very nice...interview itself was probably my best performance

Iowa (11/18)-Cold as hell in IA City...Facilities are top notch...The whole structured interview was weird...didnt do as good as I could have...but Iowa seems to be great...only drawback is its location...

Received Texas A&M Invite for Dec 8th but that is last day of classes at UT so i dunno if i will go then or reschedule for Jan 5th...cmon Galveston, SA, Southwestern....

12/2...GWU put me on hold so i guess im done...Interviewed Tuesday at SLU and thought I did well...only drawback is their facilities are older...Chicago was cold but I performed well also...have great shots at both schools...

VCU also let me know I did not get cut post interview...so hoping for acceptance their in the next 2 wks...UTMB didn't offer me an interview...im very pissed but its whatever..i guess im not cool enough for em...

12/8...i guess its official that UTSW and UTSA both dont want me...i can understand UTSW since it is a top 15 school but freakin less reputable San Antonio? A&M interview was alright...i dont take rejection well...right now i feel like i got slapped in the face by my mother...i'd understand if it was out of state..but to get dragged into the ground by your homestate while out of states are showing more love is not right...

a bit of better news...called Kentucky and they said i will get a call from the screener for an interview...they said it'll prob be in february...566 out of state applicants for 74 interviews and i get one...in texas they interview like 35% of instaters and i cant get one..

12/13-Didnt get into CMS at RFU this round...first kinda 'rejection' and it sucks...hope i get accepted in Jan/Feb

12/14-Didn't get VCU acceptance this round...fucking sucks...back to studyin for finals..

12/23-Woke up and checked my e-mail to see that SLU accepted me..my first acceptance..what a relief..i'll be a Doctor...

1/25-int at both drexel and temple yesterday...drexel is pretty nice but temple is not for me..not feeling that shady neighborhood...

5/15-waiting on the waitlist sucks but i've never been a patient kinda guy...hoping to get love from Miami or Tufts...
