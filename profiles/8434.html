Summary of Application Experience

6/11 AMCAS submited
6/15 Transcript Sent
7/9 primary application processed and verified, finally!

7/10 received secondary from Pitt, getting anxious about secondaries just by reading SDN forums, aaaahhh!!!

7/17 Submitted my first secondary (NYU) today, it didn't require extra writing as long as i pay $100. gonna try to sit down and start writing seriously for secondaries tonight!

07/23 Finished a rough draft for Baylor and GWU, waiting for editing and scratching my head really hard for the 'diversity' essay...ahhhh!

7/25 Received email from NYU confirming that they have received everything, waiting for interview begins!!! WashU Seattle also sent an email. apparently they screen because i will be notified if i get the supplement app or not...waiting...

7/27 Submitted Baylor and BU today. I had a couple people reading it, but decided not the wait for the third person to finish editing. Early submission or better essays? just exactly how much do those words weigh?

07/31 getting really nervous, couldn't decide between spending more time editing my secondaries or just send them in. submitted Pitt and Mt Sinai tonight. checking out SDN religiously and wondering if i should add more lower-tier school???!!!

08/02 totally freaking out about Stony Brook's first questions, 'In your opinion, what contemporary medical issue needs to be addressed in the U.S. healthcare system and why?' can't use my other essays to fit into this one...

08/15 Completed most secondaries, except the Stony Brook one. so the long wait for interview invites begins....

09/11 I guess application stuff happens in pairs, got rejection from Georgetown AND interview invite from Columbia today.

10/19 I was passaging cells in the sub-basement, feeling a little bit gloomy because the week was coming to an end and I feel left out among the other premeds who had gotten acceptance on Monday. and I took a minute to check my email, there it was, acceptance from TEMPLE! I am going to be a doctor!

11/19 Mt Sinai acceptance, I guess 19 is gonna be my new lucky number!! :-)

01/09 Phone call from BU interviewer!! Nine is officially my new favorite number.

03/05 Got the waitlist letter from NYU in the mail today, but I already knew it was going to happen since I saw acceptances were sent out via email on SDN forums. Pretty bummed about it, because I thought NYU was my best interview by far, I guess the great conversation was deceptive. But I couldn't stop asking, what went wrong?? I really liked NYU too. le sigh...
