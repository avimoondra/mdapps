Summary of Application Experience

I was a traditional applicant who majored in Biochemistry at Oakland University. I did not know what to expect this application cycle and was very uninformed about the process.

After my interviews Case Western Reserve is my #1, Boston University is my #2 and I have no idea how to rank my other acceptances/waitlist offers.

I made this account so that other Oakland University pre-med students would be able to have a realistic view on where they can apply and what schools may not be a fiscally responsible choice. I truly regret the amount of schools I applied to, but at the end of the day I will get to be a doctor in 2016!!!
