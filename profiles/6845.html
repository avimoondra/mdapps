Summary of Application Experience

Kaplan Total: $1,249
MCAT Total: $520
AMCAS Total: $550
Secondary App. Total: $1,035
Approx. Shipping Costs: $60
Approx. Transportation/Hotel Costs: $1,300

Grand Total: $4,714

Things I would do differently:
1. Not take the Kaplan course-I know that this might be the only option for some people, but the best practice I got was working with a study buddy and going through practice tests and sections with each other. And, it was a LOT cheaper!
2. Stick with my MCAT dates-I ended up switching my dates twice which was more money, and I didn't end up studying that much more.
3. Cut some schools out-This would've been easy for me because I had to apply to places where my fiance could either get a job or go to law school, which drastically cut down my choices. I think I could've gotten the list down even more. Probably not a possibility for many others.
4. Gotten the primary and secondaries done WAY EARLIER-This was my biggest problem throughout the entire application process. I am such a perfectionist+procrastinator which is a bad combination.
5. Waited on my top choice-When USF told me I had a strong chance of being pulled from the waitlist, I started doubting and eventually put down a deposit on an apartment in Columbia and made the decision to attend there. A week later, I got the call from USF. It's disappointing, but I think I'm in the right place! Go Tigers!
