Summary of Application Experience

Second year to apply, My GPA is poor , have lots of hours from late 80's that will take numerous hours to pull up. Have strong work background in nursing that I believe helped. I applied early, my applications were processed by the first week of June for both DO and MD. First interview in August, then OCT,then Nov, accepted at all 3. Now must make decision as to where. WV DO was great campus, new facilities but was rather expensive for out-of-state. Kirksville is also great, great reputation and I have seen a lot of their graduates in nice ER residencies.The people there were also beyond nice, got a call at home for admission dean, which I missed but they left their home number to call back. Very friendly , helpfull and treat you with respect. ETSU was the same, extremely helpful and friendly, honest about process and school. Wish I could blend these two schools into one. Location of ETSu is great for wife and kids.
Also has good reputation, small class size, and incorporates early hands on with simulator labs and clinicals. The access to hospitals and patints during the clinical years in the tri-cities area also seems very good.
My advice is apply early, keep taking classes. In hind-sight , I should have taken a recent Biochem class and also used a study course for MCAT. My saving grace was faith in God in knowing that the job change was His plan too.
