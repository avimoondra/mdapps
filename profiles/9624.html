Summary of Application Experience

6/27: I'm hoping to submit my primary AMCAS app by early July...Stuck on my personal statement...
7/7: I think my PS is ALMOST done...
7/15: It's getting closer! Out for last round of reviews...
7/24: Submitted my AMCAS application, and joining the long que for verification...also received my first secondary today, from USF.
8/18: My AMCAS went from 'Ready to Review' to 'Verified' in less than an hour...wow. Now bracing for the onslaught of secondaries...
8/21: The onslaught has begun!
8/24: Well, I've completed three and slowly working away at the others. I'm actually enjoying this quite a bit, now if I can just get one done a night!
9/11: Two more submitted today, 5 left to go!
9/13: C'Mon UCSF! I'm willing myself not to check the status page every hour...
9/20: UCLA and Stanford submitted, only 4 left to go!
9/21: First interview invite, Baylor! (if only you could see me grinning like a baby on the other side of this screen)
9/29: Done with all 13 secondaries! (Withdrawing from JHU, no word from UCSF)
10/4: Ouch! Decision deferred (pre-interview hold) at UF...anyone know the prevalence/hope of getting an interview after this? And UF is one of my state schools!
10/4: Miami interview! Woo-hoo! Ok, this makes UF's e-mail feel a little bit better!...and UCLA...ok, this day is officially better!
10/5: Helloooo, FSU!
10/11: Just got the e-mail today from UCSD! Hmm, I remember a lot of advisors telling me applying to Cali schools as an OOSer was a lost cause...just goes to show you, apply where you want to go and let everything else fall as it may!
10/12: USF! Looking forward to touring Tampa General!
10/17: First interview done...second will be rounding out the week. Feels damn good to have gotten my interview jitters out!
10/17: Just got word, my lab's research pub has now been officially accepted, and to glowing reviews! Woo-hoo!
10/22: Back from Baylor--'wow' is all I can say. I think I was sold on just about everything--happy students, flexible curriculum, awesome clinical exposure, lots of research, 18 mo. curriculum--Baylor is definately at the top of my list right now.
10/24: WOW this was an eventful day...accepted to FSU COM, and offered an interview at HARVARD and University of Florida! And it's not even a Wednesday!
11/3: This has been a slooooow week. Which I suppose I should be grateful for, as I'm traveling all over the country in short order over the next two weeks for a spat of interviews. But I still miss the Wed/Thurs rejoicing to new interview invites!
11/17: Back home after traveling to four cities in two weeks for interviews...bed and home-cooked meals never felt so good!
11/21: Got the official rejection letter from UCSF last night...12 weeks after they received my AMCAS.
11/22: After a long day in the field, I had an e-mail waiting for me from Emory to interview, woo-hoo!
11/26: This has been the first weekend in God-knows-how-long that I've actually been able to relax and play (which for me meant cooking dinner for friends, watching some football, and getting my hands dirty restoring a pond in my backyard). I can get used to this!
11/29: Acceptances to both Miami and USF!
12/8: It's been a quiet week. I'm gearing up for what in all likelihood will be my last two interviews, Emory and UF, next week...and looking forward to some time to settle back into home over the holidays. Now comes the waiting...
12/12: Just got back from Emory and absolutely LOVED it. The new curriculum is right up my alley, the Druid Hills area of Atlanta is beautiful, the students seem chill and happy, and Grady is, of course, amazing.
12/20: An acceptance letter from UF! Wow, this is a surprise!
4/12: So it's down to UF versus UM, pending an acceptance off the waitlist at Emory or UCSD (and faint, faint hope for Baylor...7 months and no word is not the best sign).
5/14: I am headed to Miami!!! :)
5/15: Take that back....I just got the call from Baylor...wow!
5/15 (cont): Ok this has been a long night, I just got the nod from UCSD as well. They are starting a program in health disparities! This is going to be a hard decision!
5/23: It's officially Baylor, folks! Best of luck to all of you still waiting for your dream school or just starting to dream of it...I hope my story helps a little!
