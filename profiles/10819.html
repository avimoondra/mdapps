Brief Profile:
So I graduated a few years ago with a degree in Economics, going to school in the Silicon Valley sucked me into business...evil, I tell you. After graduation, I worked for a high tech company and quickly decided to go back to school to do my post-bacc.

Now I am an ER Tech for local hospital (quite humbling, yes), still shadow an ED physician, volunteer as a clinic coordinator/Spanish translator for a local outreach clinic, and I am passionate about international medical outreach work (I've been to Trinidad, Guyana, Ecuador...So. Africa is next!). I was a PeaceJam Youth Mentor, a PDX Marathon Finisher(!), and graduated summa cum laude, baby!

My MCAT was less than stellar (way less), but I spent a lot of time building good relationships in the community and with my professors, both at SCU and OSU, that I believe it was my essays, letter of recs, clinical experience, and that post-bacc GPA that really helped me in this process.
