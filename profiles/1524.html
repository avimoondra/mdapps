Summary of Application Experience

Update January 2013: App cycle has been slow to this point, and looks like my interviews will be ending next week with my Case interview. I will say that I have been disappointed to get so few interviews, and probably would've had more luck if I had applied to MSTPs instead of just MD. I called one school's admissions office where I was rejected, and the lady on the other end kindly looked over my file for 5 minutes and critiqued it... she said the glaring hole in my application was a lack of service-oriented extracurriculars.

2/2/2013: This thread on SDN really made me rage that I didn't apply disadvantaged http://forums.studentdoctor.net/showthread.php?t=979634. I don't know how much it would've helped, but it definitely would've given me some extra space to discuss my family, my background, and why I didn't really go to a reputable college. Oh well, live and learn.

5/18/2013: Still on Case waitlist. I sent a LOI on 5/3, and I am 99% sure they already re-reviewed my file. Basically means they are not interested in me.

I need to reapp... worst year ever. I will be making a new mdapps in a few months. This profile is not really anonymous at this point.
