Summary of Application Experience

I really liked University of Colorado. Nice students and brand new campus. The close proximity to some of the best skiing in the country didn't hurt either!!!
Miami wasn't as bad as I feared. Great teaching hospital. Excellent curriculum. A bit big for my taste. They do have the most amazing gym in the civilized world!!! If only UofM wasn't in Miami.

As much as Miami was not my favorite school at least it would have been a school. I guess that this shows that a good MCAT is not a guarantee of admission. Now I have to figure out what to do with the rest of my life (other than cry and drink!).
