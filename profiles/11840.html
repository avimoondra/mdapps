Summary of Application Experience

November 3rd, 2004 - 8:05am PST
MSUCOM called and offered acceptance! My first, and a great school!
All downhill from here right? w00t!

February 2nd, 2005 -
EVMS is awesome! Best school I've seen in the last two years!
Interview went well, the facilities are superb, the curriculum is outstanding, and most important to me...the students are happy!
If I'm accepted, I'm going here!
Please let this go right!

February 18th, 2005 -
Just got back from interviews at PCOM and UMDNJ-SOM, EVMS waitlist waiting for me on the table. I'm not out yet! I know they take many from the waitlist, but I have to wait until Monday or Tuesday to call and see if I'm in the top 1/3. If I'm top 1/3, I've got a decent shot, send me some luck! Quick update: Called and in the top 1/3rd!!!

March 1st, 2005 -
Called EVMS to see if they were going to invite me to the second look weekend, nope. I've been bumped down to the top of the middle third. Sending in a letter of intent, letters to my interviewers, and a new letter of recommendation. Making sure things are good on my end with this one.
Wish me more luck!

March 8th, 2005 -
Turns out there's a Preview Weekend at EVMS. A couple of the first year students have extended invitations and I'll be attending. Should be great! Hoping I'll have the chance to talk to the Dean or at least members of the Admissions Committee. No to mention that the East Coast is just amazing in the Spring :)

April 29th, 2005 -
Called EVMS for my weekly update and found out I've been bumped up the top 1/3rd!!! Was so excited I was shouting at traffic from my car in Manhattan Beach (just a visit, don't live there). Calling back on Monday to confirm as my cell's signal was pathetic. Very elated and feel hopeful!
