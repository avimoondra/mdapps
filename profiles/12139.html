Summary of Application Experience

Summary:
Primary Application (submitted 7/1) - 30 schools
Secondaries - all received, but only returned 24 secondaries
Interviews - 12 interviews (2 acceptance, 2 waitlists, 3 rejections, 5 declined), 12 post-secondary rejections

***Pittsburgh explicitly stated that if I withdrew from UCI, I would be accepted to Pittsburgh (due to the post-May 15th policy). Without any information on financial aid, I declined.

This process is SO freaking random (and long). Just remember that, and don't take anything personally. And for all the obsessive people (like me) out there, yeah, I put in every last date for y'all.

For all future applicants:
If you're not sure about the strength of your application, DEFINITELY get your primary application in before Independence Day, and then finish all your secondaries before you go back to school. Be organized - this will definitely help you once you start a new semester. It'll be a long wait, but at least you're not filling the time frantically filling out applications.
