Summary of Application Experience

So, I am fairly nontraditional. I never wanted to go to grad school (hence the low undergrad gpa and no sciences!) and i haven't wanted to be a doctor since i was 15. After 2 years in Mali though, I changed my mind.

I decided before I started the application process that I only wanted to apply to DO programs. With a ton of state schools, my chances of getting into one of them is higher but I thought about it and really don't want to be an MD. I was raised around DOs, adore them as people and doctors and can't imagine myself as an MD. C'est comme ca.

They didn't received my money order till July 31, they are claiming a two week processing time :( Then on to verfication! (I had to send a MO since I received a fee waiver)

Keep checking on all aspects of App since after all my transcripts were in, AACOMAS decided that one of the transcripts was for a different campus and they were still 'missing' one of my ACTUAL transcripts (which for the two week before this, they had marked as received!) One email and three days took care of that.
