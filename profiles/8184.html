Summary of Application Experience

Submitted August 20th, yet to be verified as of 09/13/07.

09/14/07 processed and verified.

10/17/07 Last week got the email from mayo- not selected for further perusal. meh. slogging through secondaries. but wisconsin and loyola both screen and requested 2os from me, so maybe I'm not totally undesirable. 7 2os finished, and sent but for LORs (philosophy lor is hanging out in the career center, processing). Only Vandy to still hear from re: 2os, and only 9 more 2os to go. Right? Only?

11/05/07 May have secondary burnout. Sent Tufts, Loyola, u chicago and michigan in the last week or so, and it's getting harder to write thos essays. Slacking in classes, have exams this week, this process is out of control. still haven't heard from vandy, probably won't get offered secondary/interview, but it's ok. With only 4 to go, I might just stop now and wait.

11/07/07 I know that everyone gets rejected from BU. But seriously? rejection sucks. I'll get used to it. New mantra: it only takes one.

11/26/07
Christmas is about a month away, not that that means anything. Except I've been rejected from 4 schools and have heard nothing from the rest, so I finished georgetown's secondary. May as well do this, if I'm doing this. The dumbest stuff trips me up in this process. I write kick ass essays and then can't get my photo to resize properly. But today I met with a career advisor about contingency plans. RFU.

12/06/07
I kind of hate my life right now. The weather only gets gloomier and more rejections come through. If I make it through this semester I will be A)pleasantly surprised B)sleeping for ten days straight.




2/20/08
They say a watched pot never boils. Now, empirically I know this to be untrue, but a watched school hasn't shown me love in this cycle. The upside? I am getting *some* love. Minnesota-TC (where I have ties) was great, the visit was truly wonderful I got to sit in on a few classes and an M&M because the hospital is the school. The students were totally friendly and I think I did ok on the interview. There were a LOT of ethics questions, and issues of malpractice came up. But I stuck to my guns (patient doctor relationships and health autonomy therein) and I'd love to call Minnesota my school next year.

Also Albany sent me an email.

3/10/08

I canceled my interview at Albany. I don't want to live in Albany. I have some options for next year (not med, yet, but phd in neuroscience (!!!!!)) that allow me to stay in the midwest, and right now that looks pretty good to me. If I end up getting a phd I'll be well poised to apply to med school in the future if that ends up being what I want more. Ros Franklin invited me to interview.... still confirming the date.



10/2008

I was accepted at Rosalind Franklin a few weeks after my interview. Around the same time I was put on the alt list for UMinnesota-TC and withdrew from it immediately.
