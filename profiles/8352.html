Summary of Application Experience

I did a lot of research and took the hardest courses my grad school had to offer. Did ground breaking research as well which led to great LORS. I even had the degree coordinator at the time write me an LOR. I know my MCAT wasn't great at the time but it really was my only weakness in my application so I guess the rest of my application made up for it (took last paper MCAT exam offered). I had three top choices which was UWisc, UIowa, and UCinci. They're my top choices due to faculty there that do research in relation to what I was doing with my PI at the institution I got my grad degree from. I am split between UCinci and UIowa at this time but will see how the application process ends. I am already happy to get an acceptance from one of my three top choices already.
