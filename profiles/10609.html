Summary of Application Experience

I'm a graduate student in the Sage Graduate Schools Physical Therapy Doctoral program with a graduate GPA of 3.65
My AACOMAS BCPM: 3.40 Overall (including graduate): 3.45
Post-Bac work I had a 4.0 in all of my pre-med courses. Which as you can see didn't bring up my GPA as much as I had hoped.
Clinical Experience at UNC Chapel Hill Rehab for 8 weeks, one of THE best experiences ever.
I should have retaken the MCAT this fall 05 but since I got married on 13 August and left for Greece on 15 August I won't be around. I have confidence that my graduate coursework and clinical experience will help me plus I applied super early.
03 May 2005: Began filling out AACOMAS and AMCAS online.
03 June : Submitted both AACOMAS and AMCAS.
13 June : AMCAS Processed, MCAT Scores rec'd 16 June.
16 June : AACOMAS Processed and MCAT scores rec'd.
24 June : Rec'd first secondary from UMDNJ-SOM via snail mail!
15 July : Rec'd U of Buffalo supplemental via e-mail.
20 July : Rec'd U of Rochester supplemental via snail mail.
22 July : Rec'd Upstate Medical supplemental invite via snail mail.
25 July : Rec'd LECOM-Erie secondary via snail mail.
02 August: Rec'd UNECOM and Albany Med via e-mail.
Now the next round of waiting begins....Interview invites or...rejections!
17 August: Honeymoon in Greece day 2: Interview Invite to UNECOM!!! Yay! Top Choice!
24 August: I'm still in Greece, heard that I rec'd something from NYCOM saying that they've rec'd my application and that they don't start interviews until November.
12 September: Rec'd HOLD letter from Upstate. C'est la vie.
13 September: Interviewed at UNECOM. Great place! Loved it there! Nice, VERY laid back interview. Hit it off really well with my interviewer. Now, the 4-6 week wait b/c this year they've changed their admissions process. How lucky for us.
29 September: Rec'd my first rejection from AMC. I'm not surprised and not going to lose sleep over it. So much for the LOR from one of their prized Alums.
20 October: Still no word from UNECOM. UMDNJ hasn't given me any love either. NYCOM, I realized I forgot to send my LOR's to them. Did that the other day. Hoping to receive some love from them soon. I'm going to call and check up on UMDNJ and NYCOM.
29 October: Rec'd interview invite from NYCOM for 30 November! Yay!
31 October: UNECOM: still no word, UMDNJ-SOM...ditto
05 November: UNECOM ACCEPTANCE LETTER via snail mail!!! Holy Schnikes, I'm gonna be a DOctor!
11 November: Made my decision to accept my acceptance at UNECOM and withdraw my application from the other colleges I applied to.
29 November: Mailed out the 1st deposit of $500. Not looking forward to the $1000 deposit due 60-days from the time they receive this one...
