Summary of Application Experience

5/02/07 - Going to University of Illinois in Chicago.

4/10/07 - Withdrew from Cincy and rejected from Rush. Pretty much committed myself to going to school in the Chicago area.

3/26/07 - Assuming I got rejected from everywhere else but still holding out a little hope at Loyola and Rush.

3/17/07 - Waitlisted at NYMC; withdrawing.

2/27/07 - Waitlisted at NYU. Not that surprised.

2/26/07- Withdrew from Drexel waitlist. No sense in holding on to the spot.

1/31/07 - Waitlist at University of Cincinnati. Not happy but not angry either.

1/30/07 - Rejections are finally pouring in, including one from my soon to be alma matter.

1/9/07 - Interviewed at Cincinnati today and the school exceded my wildest expectations. The school has jumped to the top of my list and am hoping to hear good news from them.

12/18/06 - Great news! I was accepted at University of Illinois today. I now have options as to which medical school I go to next year. This is a fantastic early Christmas present.

12/17/06 - Waitlisted at Drexel. Was hoping for better and is a good humbling experience about this whole process.

12/14/06- NYMC interview was today. Westchester is a nice area and the college is very nice. The anatomy lab was especially good. I don't like the fact that I have to wait 10 weeks for a decision though.

12/8/06 - The wait is killing me to hear back from schools I've interviewed at. I am reminded, though, that I am blessed to have interviewed at so many schools.

12/6/06 - Double dose of good news today. Received my interview to University of Cincinnati this morning. I also interviewed at UIC and enjoyed myself! I'm hoping to hear back from my state school soon and am praying for the best.
11/20/06 - Today was a phenomenal day. I first interviewed at NYU and was SO impressed by the school and the facilities. I hope one day to go to the school and work out of Bellvue. After my interview, I received the fantastic news that I was accepted to Rosalind Franklin!!!!! I'm going to be a medical doctor. The feeling is surreal and the fact that I'm going to medical school next year relieves so much pressure. Words cannot describe how I feel right now.

11/10/06 - Had my interview at Rosalind Franklin. Thoroughly impressed by the school and look forward to hearing from them. Also received an interview from NYU! I'm so happy!

11/08/06 - Interview invite from University of Illinois. I'm so freakin' excited!

10/31/06 - Interview invite from Rosalind Franklin! Happy Halloween!

10/23/06 - Second interview invite from New York Medical College!

10/13/06 - Drexel interview today. I love the school and look forward to hearing from them.

10/3/06 - Rejected from Mayo. This one stings.

9/25/06 - First interview at Drexel!

9/21/06 - Phone interview for Mayo Medical School was today. Don't think it went too well but we'll see.

9/12/06 - First post-secondary rejection from Boston University. Just looking for one acceptance.

8/28/06 - Twenty-eight applications completed. The end is near. Also received a phone interview at Mayo; I feel very honored.

8/19/06 - First rejection from Indiana University. Actually feels good because I know that this process is finally underway. I expect many more rejections but all that matters is the one acceptance.

8/9/06- Sixteen applications completed and many more to go.

7/25/06 - AMCAS verified.

7/6/06 - I sent out my AMCAS application to a LOT of schools. Most schools are reach in my opinion. I feel my best chances are in-state but we will see how the process goes. Any comments are appreciated.
