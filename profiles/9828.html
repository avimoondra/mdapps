Summary of Application Experience

6/26/06 - Sent out AMCAS.

6/27/06 - Requested transcripts from UCSD and JC. (...taking a long time to get there.)

7/16/06 - AMCAS is ready for review

8/7/06 - AMCAS has verified the application, but there are errors in GPA calculations. Requested corrections same day. Should expect completion within 1-2 business weeks. =(

8/9/06 - First rejection. Virginia Commonwealth University.

8/19/06 - Finished second MCAT. Now I can focus on more secondaries. AMCAS doesn't seem to be correcting the application. I'll check on them again within another week.

8/27/06 - AMCAS doesn't seem to know how to add semester hours. (3 + 1.7 + 1.7 = 6.4, not 7.4). Another Academic Change Request has been sent and now I'll have to wait for another 15 days. Arrggghhh.

8/28/06 - Virginia Commonwealth University is reconsidering the application once the AMCAS changes are made. EDIT: rejected.

9/1/06-9/11/06 - Got back from a vacation. Getting back to work on some more secondaries.

10/14/06 - Last minute decision to send TMDSAS. Just sent it as of 12:30pm. We'll see what happens. (Will finish the secondaries for those schools today as well.) MCAT score came in...improved a bit, but not what I wanted, which was 35+ from the practice tests. Ended up getting PS 11, VR 10, BS 11, WS: M, which is better than the PS 10, VR 9, BS 9, WS: P that I got last year.

11/04/06 - Another last minute school: Creighton. Thank God the secondary is short. Since I've got four rejections and no interviews yet (not even secondaries from the UCs), I'm getting paranoid. Might want to throw in some more schools to increase the odds, even if it is still late. This makes the total number 41 schools now. Another thought is DO also; will have to look into it.

Sent in a letter to the UCs asking for secondaries; might give me an edge on interviews.

Sent a letter explaining grades and personal problems during first two years of college to remaining med schools.

12/18/06: Updated my GPA.

12/30/06: UCSD rejected my request for a reevaluation.

2/8/07: Received my first invite for Meharry for Feb. 16.

3/30/07: Called some schools today...Morehouse said a letter would be coming in the mail (most likely a rejection since I was on hold with them); NJMS rejected; Meharry has placed me on a waitlist.
