Summary of Application Experience

8/10 update: I was accepted to Wayne in June but realized that Michigan State (CHM) was a much better fit for me so I withdrew from Wayne. I start orientation in a week and am excited/anxious to get started! Feel free to contact me if you have any questions about my application process or comparing any schools where I interviewed. Best wishes to everyone in their pursuits!

10/29 update: So, I got accepted to the school of my dreams (MSU-CHM)! I withdrew my 2 upcoming DO interviews at PCOM and TOUROCOM-NY, but for the sake of curiosity and an excuse to visit New Orleans, I've decided to go ahead with my Tulane interview on November 30th and just check it out. I did officially accept the admission from MSU-CHM, but luckily they are flexible and have a refundable deposit until May, so that gives me some freedom to check out any other schools to make sure that I have looked at all my options. Anyway, it's a great relief! Oh also, I got waitlisted at CCOM (I hear they're pretty competitive right now) and Wayne (where's the love? I'm in their grad program!!), but all that matters is MSU at this point, and I love it.

10/19 update: I am absolutely in love with Michigan State CHM (the MD school) and had an amazing time on my interview day there. At this point, that is my dream school. I decided to cancel my TOUROCOM-NY interview because of my overly crowded schedule with school and volunteering, plus I'd prefer MSUCOM (where I was already accepted) over Touro if i go the DO route. Also, I didn't like the fact that Touro has a $2000 non-refundable deposit due 2 weeks after acceptance.. that's a little extreme, in my opinion. I also got my Nova acceptance recently but am definitely more interested in MSUCOM at this point. Still, the MD school at Michigan State is my top choice. I should be hearing from Wayne on 10/24, and am hoping for good news from Michigan State CHM soon! Once I get a better sense of where I stand with these other schools, I'll figure out whether I want to go ahead with my November interviews at PCOM and Tulane.

10/10 update: After almost sleeping through my interview (yikes! it was a mad rush to school-- luckily I live 5 minutes away), I had a fun time interviewing at Wayne. It was great to totally be in my own element, since it's my grad school, plus I was accepted to MSUCOM today (no interview necessary)!! I'm visiting their MD school tomorrow for an interview and plan to go back to campus to see the DO school in November and make my decision. I'm still keeping my options open and interviewing at schools for now. But it's official-- i'm going to be a doctor!!!!

10/2 update: Double whammy this morning-- two interview invites! (Tulane & Michigan State MD) I still haven't heard from Nova yet, but according to sources from SDN, I may just be receiving a letter in the mail instead of a call. CCOM interview was very relaxed and a lot of fun - it was eery how nice everyone was there. I should hear back from them by the end of the month. I'm also hoping to hear about interviews from Touro-NY and NYCOM this month because October is when they notify people about interviews and I was told that all those who fill out Touro's secondary are invited to interview.

9/17 update: My interview at Nova was a lot of fun! It's not my first choice but it was a great initiation into the interview process, plus I got to stay with a fabulous student host. I definitely recommend applicants stay with students whenever possible - you save money and get incredible insight on the school. I should hear back from Nova by 9/25, but even if I get accepted, I'll wait to make any decisions until I have a few more interviews. I also just got another interview invite from Wayne, my current grad school. Exciting, and a very important one!

8/31 update: My July 24th MCAT went well and I went from a 12VR, 9BS, 9PS, P to the score listed on my profile, so I am definitely happy with that. I think I may actually have a shot with some MD schools now.. we'll see as I wait to hear back from them (will take a little longer because many MD schools were holding apps until updated scores were received). I have 2 interview invites so far (both DO), and all my apps are complete so now we play the waiting game! Luckily I'm starting school next week so that will keep me busy and give me less time to be anxious about hearing back.
