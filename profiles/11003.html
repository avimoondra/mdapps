10/30
---
Just got back from Creighton Interview. Really nice school. Omaha is surprisingly cosmo and not the empty farmlands that I had imagined it to be. The students are really great and the atmosphere is really collegial. The school's primary focus is primary care which is nice, as you do clinical work from year 1. I would not mind going to school here here but my personal preference is somewhere with a good balance of research and care.

9/1
---
Took the August 2004 MCAT on a whim because I was curious. Princeton Review was amazing in helping me relearn (and learn for the first time) subjcts I haven't officially touched in 6-7 years.

Did most of my BPCM at UC Berkeley in 1997-1999. Didn't do quite as well since I wasn't focused back then. Basically my crappy undergraduate GPA was because of those 2.5 years at Berkeley. My Santa Clara and Stanford MS GPAs was in the 3.5-3.6 range.

Since I am an older applicant, I did the shot gun approach and basically just went down the list of the US News report. So far it's been send-and-wait.

I'm a good writer and I have been told by the 3 professors writing my LOR that my primary essay was interesting and a highly enjoyable read.
