Interview impressions from 9/25-9/29:
VCU: I liked this school a lot, my interview went very well!
NYMC: I didnt like the school at first, but I enjoyed it more after the tour and such. I think I just had a bad host experience. Interview also went very well.
Gtown: this school was pretty chill, except my interview went horrible! He wasnt very interested in anything I had done and it was just kind of quite for most of the time. I did not like the schools anatomy lab either, kind of a dark and danky place. I think I would like to see this school again if I get accepted, just to make sure I want to go.
Vandy: School is amazing!! The anatomy lab is superb, the campus is awesome, and all the hospitals rock my socks. Nashville is also awesome, I showed up a few days early to enjoy the town. My interview on the other hand :(! I am really bummed about this, I was praying for it to be my best experience. The interviewer was just fine, I just told him my research interests were limited and I was more interested in clinical care. He basically told me Vanderbilt makes good doctors but its focus is on research....saying that if the adcoms like me I really need to evaluate if i want to go there. =/, not a good sign. Too bad....great school. But I had to be honest with him, and I guess he was honest with me.

I am still waiting for nymc and gtown, plus i got to cancel Wisconsin. I predict yes from nymc and a no or waitlist from gtown (based of of my interviews). Trick or Treat!
