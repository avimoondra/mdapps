Summary of Application Experience

Most Impressive Interview Day: Michigan, with Duke a very close second.

Worst Interview-Related Experience: That $55 hotel before the CWRU interview. Oh, that was scary and cold.

Best Interview Day Food: Michigan, if you include the night-before Pizza House dinner. Otherwise, OUWB.

Best Acceptance Notification: The text message ten minutes before midnight on October 15th instructing me to meet Michigan medical students on the U-M diag. This was followed by much hugging, near tears, XL UMMS T-shirts, and a very early a.m. call to my parents.

Friendliest, Most Welcoming Admissions Staff: Michigan & OUWB (tie), though all schools were great in this respect.

Most Impressive Hospital: The Cleveland Clinic, obviously.

Prettiest Campus: Duke

Nicest Medical School Building: Duke

Interview Pen Rank: Pitt > OUWB > MSU > CCLCM > Wayne > Michigan = CWRU > Duke (no pen)

Best Second-Look Swag: Michigan (Bag, T-shirt, water bottle, pin, pen, and a maize and blue football. Yes, a football.)

Worst Interview Decision: That Chipotle burrito before the Duke interview. I felt terrible, and I think I had food poisoning.

Greatest Failure to Learn From My Mistakes: Another Chipotle burrito the night before my Pittsburgh interview.
