Summary of Application Experience

R- Received Secondary
S- Secondary Submitted
C- Complete
H- Application on some kind of hold

TIMELINE

5/29/2007 Transcripts received

6/14/2007 Submitted AMCAS

6/18/2007 AMCAS Processed

8/06/2007 Letters of Recommendation sent from school via VirtualEvals

8/20/2007 ALL SECONDARIES SUBMITTED....and an INTERVIEW from Tulane.

8/27/2007 Put on hold from VCU, saying committee needed several weeks to determine if I would get an interview. I am assuming this means they want to see grades from my graduate program.

9/11/2007 After the Tulane invite, I was finally stopping the email/status-checking madness, and then BAM! EVMS interview invite. I'm really excited. I wasn't expecting to see any positive movement at all until at least January given my low GPA.

9/26/2007 Had my interview at EVMS. I really liked it and could see myself going there. The students, while really motivated and hard-working, also seemed very chill and very into being involved in other things like community service, sports, etc. The hospitals are awesome, as are the few faculty I met along the way.

10/4/2007 Finally called Loyola to check on my status...been put on hold till the first of the year. Also checked my status at VT...also put in an interview hold. Doing this SMP may be a blessing eventually, but right now it is killing me by having so many apps on hold.

Oh the waiting...

10/11/2007 So I worked up the courage to call EVMS since they said we could call two weeks after our interview to check on the committee's decision, and HOLY CRAP! After much holding, I was told I would be receiving a 'letter'....with a dramatic pause...'of acceptance'. I'M GOING TO MED SCHOOL!!! Now if I could only focus for my exam tomorrow...

10/13/2007 Checked my email in the morning and good ol' BU gave me the ax. Oh well...I'll take one rejection for an acceptance any day.

10/17/2007 Seems like I am having a short trend of one piece of good news with a piece of bad. Got an interview invite from VT only to find Dartmouth's (expected) letter of rejection in the afternoon. Funny thing is that with the EVMS acceptance I was really considering withdrawing from VT, but now I figure, if I am ok with funds come December, I'll give VT a little more of a chance. (we'll see how Tulane goes in two weeks).

11/4/07 Had my interview at Tulane last week. I really loved it-I really recommend staying with a student host. Everyone I met was great, really involved with something in the community, motivated students...the 'irony' so to speak was that I felt out of my three interviews, the student interview went the least well. I chalk it up to being exhausted, but I'm a little scared it'll ruin my chances for this great school. Guess we'll see.

11/6/07 I know I shouldn't begrudge interviews, but why oh why am I getting them from schools I was considering withdrawing from...at least I know I'm definitely not going to go to USUHS. The money just isn't worth it for me.

11/26/07 WOOO! Got into Tulane! It would happen that the few days I actually don't check my mail the letter would come. Kudos to the adcom for meeting right before Thanksgiving! :)

01/11/07 So, got the waitlist from Vermont, heard back in December I was put on hold at GW...gotta get my butt in gear to send my grades from last semester (especially to the schools that requested them...), although at this point, I sort of just want to choose between Tulane and EVMS and have this all done with. Anyone have suggestions on which I should pick?

03/07/08 Rejection week it seems...still have no idea where I'm going to go. Waiting for EVMS revisit and a trip down to Tulane...as well as potentially writing a letter of interest to Vermont. I suppose I should go ahead and do it, but I'm kind of in this place where I wonder if my diminishing interest in the school is because I'm waitlisted, or that I'd really rather be somewhere else.

March is rejection month. Looks like it's all over kids...but I am definitely not complaining. I go to school with enough people who didn't even get an interview.

04/07/08 So more rejections, updated late March interview invites that I declined...went down to New Orleans for my own impromptu revisit. I don't want to jinx my EVMS revisit coming up, but I think I'll end up there.

Going to EVMS! I'm really happy with my decision. If any of you have any questions about the process, my choice, etc., feel free to leave a message! Good luck!
