Summary of Application Experience

6/6/06 I picked quite a date to submit my TMDSAS and Baylor app's. Good thing I'm not superstitious. I have had all of my transcripts sent, and three of my four letters are into the HPO. I have started working on secondaries and will get them done hopefully within a week. I'm only applying to Texas schools (6 in total I think), so hopefully I'm not taking a risk by applying to more schools. I just don't want to apply to schools for the name, fork over secondary application money, and then have them waitlist me. I also didn't apply to schools that I probably wouldn't go to if I did get in. I guess we'll see how it goes!

6/20/06 AMCAS has been processed and sent. MCAT is in....about what I expected. Not as good as I should've done, but good enough I hope. All letters are in and I'm waiting for our HPO to send them. Working on secondaries.

6/21/06 TMDSAS has been processed and sent. Baylor secondary is done. I'll get around to UTSW, A&M, and Tech soon.

070806 I have an interview at UT-Houston 0802! Tech's secondary is done. I hate my essay for UTSW, so it's taking awhile. It will be done. By Tuesday. Or else.

071106 Ha! Got those damn secondaries done and submitted! Hooray! So, unless I change my mind and decide to apply to Tulane as well (I'm toying with the idea), applications are DONE! Oh, and I received an interview at UTMB, so that means I've got dates at two of my top public schools choices.

071106 (Later, same day) Alright, I'm applying to Tulane. The secondary was pretty easy, so I'm mailing it tomorrow. Also, got an interview at Tech....I know a lot of pre-meds knock this school, but until I have some acceptances, I have no right to turn my nose up at any school that's interested in me. Also, I've heard they have nice facilities and their invitation was the most helpful/informative one I've received.

072406 Sheesh, after a drought of two weeks with no interviews, I received an invite from UTSA! Yay! Of course it's the Monday after we were planning on moving me from Austin, and I'm supposed to have a final, but oh well, the final will be moved.
-Later on, same day: Two invites on the same day! Hooray! A&M...supposed to be on 0803, but unfortunately, that would be 3 interviews in a row, and I'm already scheduled to fly out to Lubbock.

082106 In a two week time span, I had four interviews, a test, my final final, moved home from Austin to Houston, and graduated from college. I have decided against going to Brownsville for now, so I am in the process of looking for jobs. I rescheduled A&M until the end of the month, and am still waiting from UTSW & Baylor (I heard both are number fiends that give automatic invites if your stats are above a cutoff, and may never look at your app if you're below...I hope it's not true), and Tulane.

082506 So I had a dream last night that Tulane started sending out invites, and then today, there was an invite for me for 0921! Am I psychic? Probably not, but if I am, I'm crossing my fingers for a dream in which I find out where I'm going.... =)

092306 I just got back from Tulane today. I was pretty impressed by the atmosphere, and I thought all three of my interviews went well. I also finally received an interview invite from UTSW, so that is pretty exciting. Now if Baylor would just consider me, I'd be a happy camper.

101906 Accepted to TULANE! Hooray!

111506 A&M loves me!

111606 And Southwestern does too! This is truly a surprise! Then to come home to a letter from UTHSC San Antonio? Could it get any better?!?

011407 After last-minute visits to UTSW and UTHSCSA, I have decided to go to San Antonio, and I withdrew from all of the other Texas schools. In the end, I decided to opt for the healthier, less-competitive school. UTSW is a good school, but med school will be hard enough without having to compete with my classmates for the 20% A's.

This has been one hell of a process, so my advice to anyone is make damn sure this is what you want. I saw too many premeds who were premed because they were overachievers, had always been overachievers, and they thought that the logical thing for an overachiver to do would be to go to med school (without ever considering whether they'd actually be a good doctor). My application wasn't the best ever, but it was strong and well-rounded. Also, I like to think I have personality and people skills (one of my interviewers at UTSW said I wasn't a 'troll,' which makes me think other applicants are). I think that's why I had such success getting interviews at every school that's not a number whore (cough, Baylor, cough). Oh, and for those ladies that are interested: I wore a black pants suit to every school to which I later recieved an acceptance. I alternated with a grey skirt suit, which had 0% success. Just FYI.

If any of y'all care, I am quite happy to say that I'll be in San Antonio for the next four years, eating Taco C, shopping at HEB, and keeping my sanity while I earn my MD. =)
