Summary of Application Experience

Medical schools love people like me. Easy money for them with no hassle what-so-ever. Went down the list on the AMCAS website and selected every single school. Went down the list on the DO AMCAS website and selected every single school. Some schools were nice, and just sent me a rejection email in about 1 week, others made me waste more time and money by asking for secondaries. Working full time and going to school is not beneficial AT ALL. Take out loans, don't make the same mistake I did. But, I did get into Ross University in Dominica so I will be attending that in May. Obviously I would have loved to have attended a US school but my grades speak for themself. Have a family to support so no time for Post Bac/Masters. Transfer student from a JC to UCSB with a BS in Biochemistry. (got a few more secondaries but can't recall which ones, I guess they saw some potential in my MCAT).
