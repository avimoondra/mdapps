Thoughts:
I'm glad that this process is over. I was very lucky during the app cycle and got into many of my top choices. To those who have similar interests, just be true to yourself, and you won't be disappointed! The final 3 schools that I considered - Harvard, UMich, and UCSF - all have good reputations for fighting health and healthcare disparities. Ultimately, I chose to go to HMS based on a gut feeling; let's hope that my gut doesn't mislead me!

Advice:
To current applicants, don't obsess over M1/M2 or small differences in curricula. It's M3/M4 that truly matter, and any of the 'elite' med schools will have comparable training. Go where you feel comfortable and where you think that you'll be inspired to succeed. If you're lucky to have multiple acceptances, go with your gut!

I won't be checking this site or SDN any more for the considerable future. Good luck to applicants!
