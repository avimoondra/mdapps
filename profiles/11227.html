Summary of Application Experience

Yes, that's a lot of schools. Most are reaches, though. Transcripts recieved: early May
AMCAS submitted: 7/27


AMCAS verified: 8/10
So far, I've sent LOR's to UPenn and NYU because they only require 2 letters. In retrospect, I should have just waited on UPenn because they are non-rolling. Regardless, my 2 science LOR's will probably be my best, coming from people I knew well.
Last Update 11/15
Withdrew from many schools due to a gross lack of funds---and I mean gross.


2 batches of my LOR's weren't received by schools. By the time I found out, it was past the deadline at 2 schools, UPenn and NYU. UPenn let me fax them along with my letter office verifying the date they were originally sent. NYU told me they were too busy to even check and that they didn't care if I could verify the original date they were sent. I had already paid the fee to NYU and you might think that would buy a little consideration. I am especially bitter because I have been so broke during this process. I've had to borrow thousands! of dollars from my brother, who is still in school, just to pay rent, and NYU can't even check to see if they received my LOR's...

I finally have an interview---it's my last chance, too! Go UIC-Rockford!!!

Good luck, everyone.
