Summary of Application Experience

07/11/14 - At the time of writing this, I just submitted my AMCAs application for verification (no secondaries, rejections or II's yet). However, filling out the AMCAS, and making sure you did it exactly right, is exhausting. Tracking down contact info for every experience was a little difficult for things I did 4+ years ago! Also, it's a little scary when they say, "you can be rejected for submitting the wrong info and you cannot reapply or change any information." I now know why people say the 48,000 applicants every year is a little inflated; a good amount of those probably have things filled out improperly or get rejected from schools based on technicalities. Overall, it is exciting to finally be applying. This has been a long-time coming and I am ready to hear back from schools. To be honest, I am quite nervous given my GPA. We'll see!

11/08/14 - At this point, all secondaries have been done for at least a month and I am sitting with one interview invite (OHSU), two rejections (BU & Georgetown), and one pre-interview hold (SUNY Upstate) which basically means a rejection. In recent years, BU has only accepted 1 out of every 100 applicants. Crazy. It was really unnerving sitting with 'three' rejections and no II's for about a month. However, OHSU came through! Although it is a small victory, I feel like my hard work over the past 6 years has finally been validated. Someone actually wants to give me a shot at being a doctor! I submitted all secondaries from early September to early October. Applying to 24 schools is a lot and I think some of my secondaries show 'app fatigue.' I wish I submitted my app earlier but I was not ready. Overall, I feel like I have nothing to do because I am not slaving away every night and weekend trying to get secondaries done! Honestly, I will not apply to 5 Florida schools if I have to do this again. While the schools look interesting, most have the mission of, "Training Floridian doctors for Floridians." I really could see myself in Boca Raton, however.

12/03/14 - Great News! Got invited to an interview at FAU COM in Boca Raton! Maybe that idea from earlier will actually pan out :D. My MMI/one-on-one interview at OHSU went very well, felt very good about it. Great school, I would love to go there. Although on the other side of the country, from all outward appearances I also think going to school in tropical Florida could be a nice change of pace. I guess we'll see. It has been validating to actually get an interview from out of state, especially FAU where they only take 60 people per year, with only 25% of them being OOS. With low stats, it is easy to fall into the slump of, "I am not good enough." I started thinking, "well, my only chance is at OHSU because they favor IS." SOOOO validating getting an OOS II.

02/11/2015 - Two interviews down and I don't believe I'll receive any more...it's getting fairly late in the season. Overall, I have been surpised how things have turned out. OHSU gave me a very early interview date and FAU, who I thought I had no chance with, wanted to interview me! Similarly, tons of schools I thought I had a chance at based on stats didn't even give me the time of day. I'm on hold at OHSU until April 30th and am dying to know!

03/30/2015 - As expected, I haven't received anymore interviews. Of the two I had this season, I was put on the alternate list at FAU and am still awaiting my decision from OHSU on April 30th. So frustrating! 6 months post-interview date before I'll hear my decision...

05/02/2015 - Rejected by my home-state program OHSU on 4/28 but accepted to FAU on 4/30! All in all, this is not what expected to happen but am excited to be attending a US MD school! It's actually happening! 4 years in a different place will be good I think too...
