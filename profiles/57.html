Applying DO and MD as broadly as possible because my undergrad grades are horrific.

Mcat scores were 24 on on the old exam last may. Took the new MCAT in April, and prelim scores were in the 90th percentile.

Since I'm applying as a nontrad, several years out of graduating from college, my ECs are skewed heavily toward the gainfully employed side of things. Luckily, I found a job in a lab as an assistant, and for several years I worked as a scribe. Other ECs, I'll post up after the cycle ends.
