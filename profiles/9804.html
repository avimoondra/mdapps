Summary of Application Experience



was going to wait for MCAT scores to submit amcas, but submitted 5 days earlier than score release date.

April MCAT: 26M

6/16 - submitted AMCAS

7/5 - AMCAS verified/certified and done processing

7/5 - added 10 more schools that i have a good chance of getting into

7/5 - email from VCU about receipt of AMCAs primary, link given to status page. when i logged in, it said 'application withdrawn' which is a HUGE ERROR!

7/6 - straightened out VCU mess, and submitted MCW secondary

7/13 - submitted AACOMAS

8/16 - AACOMAS verified

8/19 - August MCAT, form BS, 28m

11/xx - Received interview invitations from VCOM and DMU

12/18 - Interviewed at VCOM - went great!

12/20 - Accepted (via telephone) for the VCOM Class of 2011

1/17 - Interviewed at EVMS (panel of 2 interviewers) - went well

1/30 - Waitlisted at EVMS (bottom 1/3) - somewhat disappointed

3/29 - Met with Dean of EVMS to show my interest in the school and to see what I could do to upgrade on the waitlist. Come to find out that I was initially evaluated with the lower of my two MCAT scores. They didn't have my higher score. Mistake was fixed...a bit too late. Still stuck on the waitlist

4/1 - Sent in update to EVMS letting them know my present dealings

4/8 - Called for EVMS waitlist status and I was informed that I was now in the middle 1/3)

5/8 - Called for EVMS waitlist status and I was informed that I was now in the upper 1/3)

5/15 - Received call from Director of Admissions (EVMS) and was offered an acceptance! :^)
