Summary of Application Experience

6/1/06: MCAT scores come out this month! I've been tinkering with the school list as of late. I really only want to apply to 12 or 13 schools. Ideally, I want to be accepted to UVA early in the season so I can be selective as to which interviews I attend. We'll see how that works out.

6/10/06: The AAMC is screwing with the score release date. Most people who called have been told 6/12 or 6/13, then they put up a msg on the website the other day that said 6/20. That better mean they're going to be in the mail on 6/20. Nowhere else in the world does it take 2 months to grade scantrons and generate a bell curve. Jeez, this is frustrating.

6/20/06: Waiting for scores is over...30P! I'm excited. No way I'm retaking.

6/27/06: I've gotten 2 secondaries (Georgetown and UK), one screened, one not. I also found the GWU secondary, so I finished that up. I feel like things are going pretty well. The Kentucky secondary, though, is killer. It has 6 short essays (a paragraph, I guess), and then 1 long one for OOS.

7/4/06: 4th of July holiday! Finally, a day off of work. That means I can write my last UK essay. I've also received the screened secondary from VCU. I just need to add a few more lines to that essay, and I'll be set with it. Hopefully, later this week when the holiday is over, I'll be getting some more. My last LOR should be on it's way to Interfolio today, too.

7/9/06: I got the UVA secondary this week. I'm finished with every secondary I have, except an in-state tuition form for UVA. Things are going well; I hope they continue to go like this. I'm waiting on 6 secondaries right now. Plus, my last LOR hasn't showed up in Interfolio yet, so I need to check on that. There's just so much stuff to keep on top of...

7/20: I finally got my last letter of recommendation submitted to Interfolio. I've gotten all my letters electronically transmitted to the schools I've received secondaries from thus far. GWU emailed me yesterday letting me know I was complete. Now I just wait...

7/26: I'm complete at almost every school I've sent my secondaries back into. All but one of the schools left on my list screens pre-secondary, so I don't know how long that's going to take. Although, it does feel good when I get screened secondaries, as opposed to 'give us money' secondaries.

7/29: Grrrrr. So, EVMS (and I'm sure UVA and VCU are to follow) has decided that I'm not a VA resident. This sucks. So, I'm a person without a state for this application cycle. I might be able to swing FL residency, but I only meet the requirements for admissions at USF and UM (no biochemistry). So, I added USF; I don't want to live in Miami. Hopefully I'll hear something from some of my schools next week, because it seems like people are starting to get interviews from GWU and Vanderbilt. I want one!

8/1: Weird status update from VCU. Not exactly sure what it means. It's not a rejection, but it certainly doesn't sound so hot.

8/9: I got my first interview!!! Thank you Kentucky. Scheduled for 9/30.

8/17: Interviews 2 & 3. Invited to ETSU and EVMS for 9/12 and 9/15.

8/31: Wow, it's hard to believe that 2 weeks from tomorrow, I'll be done with my first two interviews. Since one of them is in southern VA (EVMS), I get to go see my folks for the weekend. Other than that, it's been a pretty quiet two weeks.

9/5: I got a phone call from Mr. Larkin in Admissions at USF asking me to come to Tampa for an interview! Yay! I'm so excited. In-state tuition rocks.

9/12: I interviewed at ETSU today, and had the best time. It definitely moved up on my list of schools. Now I wait until the week of 10/16...

10/7: I've got this 'active hold' thing going on at ETSU. I'm not sure if I'm actually on hold, or if this is a result of me interviewing earlier than the 10/16 notification date. Either way, I'll continue to wait until 10/16.

10/14: Ahhhhh. Tomorrow I get a decision from USF. Right now, it's definitely in my top 3. I should also hear from UK on Monday, and ETSU (if I got accepted) by Friday/Saturday (snail mail).

10/15: I'm going to medical school!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

10/21: I've been accepted to UK COM over the phone. I'm withdrawing from all the other schools aside from ETSU and UofL. ETSU is probably my top choice. UK was in probably my #2. If I get accepted to ETSU, I'll withdraw from UofL, also.
The ETSU ADCOM meets on 10/23 to decide the first round of acceptances. They won't tell you anything over the phone, so I'll have to wait for a letter in the mail.

10/23: Got my UK letter in the mail. Now withdrawing from everywhere but ETSU (#1!!!) and UofL.

10/29: I didn't get in to ETSU; still on hold.

As of 2/5, I was still on hold at ETSU. I sent my letter to them to withdraw. I will matriculate at UK COM this summer!
