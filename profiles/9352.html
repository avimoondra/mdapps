Summary of Application Experience

8/22 - decided it was about time to make one of these to try and keep track of everything. I know I have quite a few 'reach' schools on my list, but my feeling is that I only want to do this once, so I might as well apply everywhere I can, bury myself in debt and hopefully get in somewhere.

8/24 - Wow, interview invite at NJMS, very excited.

8/29 - Out of state interview invite from Penn State, very psyched.

9/8 - received interview invite from LECOM-erie. Why do all of these osteo school feel it neccessary to assign you a date?!?

9/14 - NJMS interview yesterday. I am in love with this school, all of the students seem so happy and my interviewer was the nicest woman alive. Crossing fingers.

9/23 - Penn state interview was awesome. Such a beautiful facility and the students really love the school. Feel like my interviews went pretty good.

10/15 - got the call from Dr. Heinrich at 3pm today during the giants game!!!!! Admitted to med school AND the giants win; awesome day.

10/16 - withdrew my application from WVSOM and CCOM and cancelled the respective interviews; accepted to LECOM-erie
