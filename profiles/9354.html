Caution: these wrap-ups are written immediately post-Interview and do not always reflect my emotions post-decision

Vandy interview wrap-up: Awesome school. The interview was my first and a little laid back so i'm not sure what to make of it. I loved the students and the feel of the school though. if vandy admits me, i'm def gonna have a hard time saying no ie I might actually accept

Washu interview wrap-up: Lovely facilities. I was amazed by the faculty and buildings. However, even having grown up in STL, i'm put off by the city and some of the students I met didn't click with me. I love the administrators and research opps tho...who knows. i liked the ppl interviewing me.

Baylor interview wrap-up: I'M IN LOVE. enough said. i want to marry the school. the curriculum schedule, the students, the freedom everything is bueno. If i get in, vandy better watch their back. I really think I would fit in here and the kids i interviewed with were great. the facilities also rocked.

Emory Interview wrap-up: i'm put off by the distance between emory and grady. i like that the new curriculum is like baylor's and gives you that 6 months of freedom and school starts in august. facilities on the up and up ie new buildings for class entering in 2007(me). long ass tour, my feet still hurt. atlanta is a great city but the airport annoyed me(5 hour flight delay in air for a 45 min flight) and HUGE airport.

UTMemphis wrap-up: not bad for what i expected and for a state school where funding isn't that extensive. glad to find a lunchi could eat(subway). grilled by first interviewer. loved second interviewer.

UChicago wrap-up: awesome! it wasn't as cold as i thought. i got lost and stuck downtown and missed half of lunch but can honestly say i still loved the school. it's definetly one of my favorites. fingers crossed. i love the humanitarian mission and the opps for outreach.

Because I'm a bitter person who didn't get into vandy I'll list all the things I didn't like in the first place (the classroom buildings are crappy to me, the all day at school thing sucks, the Emphasis program was okay but I didn't like the idea of forced research, I didn't like that 40% of last year's class was from vandy/washu/notre dame, i didn't like that some people that annoy me now in undergrad are gonna be there...
