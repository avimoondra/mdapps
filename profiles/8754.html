Summary of Application Experience

BCPM GPA is low with an upward trend...I really hope this works out...

6/21 Submitted Primary to AMCAS
6/29 Verified with an error that lowers my BCPM GPA
7/05 AMCAS corrected the error
7/25 Decided to add a few more schools since I decided not to complete the secondaries for some of the very competitive schools I originally had on my list (Johns Hopkins, Columbia, Yale, Dartmouth). Added VCU, Wayne State, UMaryland, and Case.
8/08 IUSM Interview Invitation! First one! Scheduled for 9/5
08/11 Pre-secondary rejection from Wayne State. I guess I didn't really want to go there, but I still felt sad. I hope this isn't a sign of more bad things to come...
8/14 Undergraduate Institution released LORs! Now I can finally start getting complete
09/5 First interview at IUSM, I think it went pretty well, but it's always hard to tell...
09/5 Called Georgetown to check status and was told there was a letter in the mail...rejection
09/7 ON HOLD AT UCHICAGO!!! This is sad - it is my first choice and an interview would have been a dream come true, but it also seems like a minor miracle that I didn't get rejected outright...I guess I couldn't really have hoped for better given my below average stats...
09/8 Georgetown rejection letter...they were very curt.
9/10 George Washingtion interview!!! AAAAAAAAAAAAH! So happy!
9/19 Drew Secondary Received! Happy I wasn't screened out...
9/30 BU rejection email...very nice rejection...I am pretty bummed...no interview invitations in awhile, just a rejection...
10/2 Loyola interview invitation!!! Ecstatic!!!
10/12 GW Interview, feel like it would be a perfect fit for me....let's hope they agree!
10/29 Many things have happened, Indiana acceptance which I am thrilled about. Also had a Loyola interview recently, it really is the happiest place on earth and I would be thrilled to be a student there...Case rejection today...I am dissapointed, especially because they had my file for so long that I started to let myself hope that I would get an interview...oh well...
11/07 Post-interview waitlist at GW a few days ago...I am dissapointed, I really feel like I fit their mission and opportunities perfectly, but oh well...I guess everyone feels like they fit and they can only take so many people this early...I will write a letter expressing interest very soon. I have to wonder if my very awkward faculty interview had anything to do with it...though it was positive, it was very strange and only 10 minutes long...I really wish I would start hearing one way or another from more schools. I am thrilled with Indiana, but I would love to have some geographic options as my SO is also applying...
11/10 Loyola acceptance!!! AAAHHHH!!! Thrilled!!!
11/25 Things have been quiet for some time, but I got my Pittsburgh rejection today. Still hoping for a few more interviews...
12/15 Cornell rejection came in the mail...dissapointmented, but oh well...I also decided to send a new letter of recommendation to schools that I continue to be very interested in...let's see if it makes a difference...probably not...I am pretty sure the cycle is over for me...though I am hoping for a few more interviews for geographic diversity, which would give me the chance to make my personal life a lot easier for the next four years, I don't see it happening. I am not complaining, with my numbers, I am VERY lucky to have two acceptances with very little nail biting...
12/31 UIC acceptance in the mail. I cannot believe I actually have choices...
