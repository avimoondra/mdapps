Summary of Application Experience

---------------------------------------------------------------------
INTERVIEW SUMMARY

VCU/MCV - Simply awesome school with a great history and amazing admissions staff. Their application process seems really fair and they take alot of pride in the class that they put together. The interview was really laid back (I think it's what they're known for). It was with one faculty member. The student guides were really nice and helpful and the facilities are pretty outstanding. They are constantly building and expanding. The hospital system is huge! The professors and faculty are very impressive and definitely eager to help students. I hope I hear back with good news when December 15 rolls around!

George Washington - Another fantastic place! The people here are so nice! The state of the art hospital is pretty amazing. The school is known for producing great clinicians - makes sense with a such an enormous patient diversity. The med students here seem really happy and they were eager to help us learn more about the school. 2 individual interviews which were pretty laid back. One with a current student and one with a faculty member (usually a doctor). I was very impressed with the school. They say we'll hear back within 6 to 8 weeks. Hoping for good news!
