  • Accepted: Yes

Summary of Experience:

I loved Wash U! Facilities there are incredible; I especially liked the fact that you get your own study carrel first and second year. The hospitals were also amazing. Every student was incredibly friendly and many walking by would ask out of the blue how my interviews went and who I had them with. There is a wealth of research opportunities--most students do some form of research during their time there--which I like the sound of. St. Louis might not be the best city ever, but it is eminently livable and cheap and everything you might want on offer.

I never update these experience descriptions until after acceptance, because I don't want to get my hopes up.
