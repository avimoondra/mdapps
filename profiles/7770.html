Summary of Application Experience

I made this profile like megboo to keep myself motivated.
So far these are the schools I am planning on applying to. I am going all the way DO :) and truly hope to either get into DCOM, WVSOM or CCOM this year (fingers crossed).
I was always passionate about medicine and art/architecture. I wanted to be a doctor since I remember but during my freshman year in college I went thru a painful divorce which was disastrous to my GPA. I thought I had no shot getting into med school so that is how I ended up at IIT in the architecture program. Of course I only lasted 1 semester there LOL. I missed the thought of being a doctor and knew that no substitutes would make me happy, so I went back to medicine. I believe I can connect both of my passions and become a plastic/reconstructive surgeon.... but at the same time I love the idea of family practice (especially rural, cause I love mountains, horses and wild places like doctormom :D ) or sports medicine and possibility to use OMM on the daily basis (it fascinates me) so either way I will love what I do.
I changed my major to art history because I wanted to pick something that interests me rather that something that will look good on med school application.
My grades are not fantastic :( but I hope I will get some love this upcoming cycle.
It's official...the above GPA is what i ended up with after this semester. It was crazy taking 23 credit hrs but i managed to do really well :)
