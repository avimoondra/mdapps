Summary of Application Experience

7/12 Sent in primary app

9/3 First interview invites!!!!! GWU and UVM.......so excited!!!!!

9/18 wow... didnt expect to get so many more interview invites by this time!!!! Baylor, Buffalo, GWU, UVM, Indiana, UCI, Rochester, and Tufts!!!!

9/26 Finally finished all secondaries...the UC's took FOREVER. Freakin' UCSD autobio sketch was a killer!


10/5 BAYLOR = AMAZING!! Cant say enough about how wonderful and strong this program is!!

10/15....wonderful. while my friends are getting acceptances i get
a. a waitlist from GWU (inexplicable!?) and
b. a REJECTION from UVM......such a bad interview i dont blame them.

-----in retrospect, GWU probably felt i was unlikely to matriculate to their school. and they would be 100% correct about that.


11/8 LOVED UCI!!!! Wow.....love the city of Irvine. Still......im getting anxious about having no acceptances thus far.

11/19 Harvard and UCSD were OFF THE HOOK.....these may be my 2nd and 3rd favorite schools so far. Baylor still has my heart though.


11/30 Had my NYC trip--NYU, Columbia, Sinai, AE.....overall id say Columbia and NYU grabbed my attention the most. Sinai gave me a sick feeling to my stomach for some odd reason. and students didnt seem relaxed at all.

o...btw....thank you rochester for the waitlist. the feeling is mutual--didnt like it there too much. didnt feel like students were content there at all.


12/1 its now december and i have NO ACCEPTANCES, 2 WAITLISTS and a REJECTION. My chances are good, especially considering the fact that I have so many interview invites (i have NO IDEA why I am getting so many)and i know im a good interviewer. also, most of my interviews were in november, and a great many were nonrolling schools---so i must be patient. still......im anxious for that first acceptance

12/4 USC was, for lack of a better word, 'sexy'. its an attractive place to go to school. wouldnt mind going there.


12/5 Wow. I did not expect to have such a negative impression of michigan. its a wonderful program, perhaps even the 2nd best program ive interviewed at (behind Harvard), but i dunno about living in the midwest and ann arbor....ill think about this longer, and may even try to convince myself to like michigan more IF i get in (which i would be lucky to get in). if i get rejected, i wont feel too bad based on my current negative impression of the school.


12/11 UCSD ACCEPTANCE!!!! FIRST ONE!!!!WOOHOOOO I LOVED SD!!!!! WOW....and this was at the TOP OF MY LIST!..... i need to get drunk fast!!!!!

tomorrow: decline interviews from schools that i wont want to ever go to.

12/13 Had a fun session of burning all of my class/study materials from classses I hated (and had to ace bc i was premed). hehehehehe....when put my lab manuals in there, it was SOOOOO sweeett...


12/15 Got a phone call from the dean at UCI congratulating me on acceptance today!!! I should be hearing back from Indy soon, too.

time for a list of my top ten choices based on interviews:

1. Harvard (fat chance)
2. Baylor
3. UCSD (accepted)
4. Yale
5. Columbia
6. USC (accepted)
7. Michigan
8. UCI (accepted)
9. UC Davis
10. Indiana (accepted)

12/19 Mt Sinai acceptance!!!.....hmmm...well at least i know i have the possibility of going to a NYC school.

12/20 I really enjoyed my visit to Yale! Its quite a unique school...curriculum is BY FAR THE MOST LAID BACK among the schools ive seen....no grades the first two years!!??? wow. interviews went ok, i had two female interviewers who were a bit unreadable. well..guess its time to wait till march.

12/21 I think i need to be realistic about some of the schools ive interviewed at. As far as Yale, Columbia, and Harvard are concerned....i would be VERY LUCKY to get into any of those schools...most likely will get into none.

12/21 ACCEPTED TO INDIANA!!!! I really enjoyed this school. A great financial aid package could seriously bring me over there. (the next day i got the Eli Lilly Scholarship!!...wow.)

wow.....months of nothing, and then all of a sudden i get 4 acceptances in 10 days. just goes to show you that you must be patient in this process.

1/10 No word yet from other schools. Waiting for USC to come around soon....and i then the march decisions. i kinda wish nonrolling decisions were earlier--that way i can know for certain what my life is gonna be like/where its going to be for the next 4+ years. kinda sucks if know lots of people in your area and you cant even tell them where you will be stationed 8 months from now.

1/13 Im loving my year off.....omg its soo great to be accepted and not have many responsibilities!!!! I recommend taking a year out to everyone. Not to mention, most of my interviewers thought that deciding to go a year out was a very mature/logical decision. Ive got loans to pay off!!!

1/15 Albert Einstein Acceptance!........scholarship money would make this school more attractive.

1/16 USC acceptance!!!!! Fight On!!!! Du...dudu-dudu-dudu Du-du du...duduuuuu......lol

1/17 Haters beware....im a PLAYAH...and playah's dont care.

1/22 This is just ridiculous....another interview?? From Cornell no less. i just cant afford to visit any more schools on the east coast. im going to officially withdraw. gluck to whoever takes my interview spot.

1/30 Withdrew from Cornell and Mt. Sinai--nothing is too wrong with Sinai, other than fact that I know no matter what I would go to UCSD over it any day.

2/13 UC DAVIS ACCEPTANCE!!! A phone call from the dean. whoohoo!!! At least i have the option of staying close to home now.

3/3 NYU Acceptance. i guess ill make sure to withdraw quickly!


3/10 After looking at schools a bit more....new rankings:

1. Harvard (accepted)
2. UCSD (accepted)
3. Columbia (rejected)
4. Baylor (most likely rejected)
5. Yale (rejected)
6. UCD (accepted)
7. USC (accepted)
8. Michigan (waitlisted)
9. Indiana (accepted)
10. UC Irvine (accepted)


3/11 just looked in the mailbox and found my columbia rejection! well, that was expected. guess my next rejections will be harvard yale...

3/17 Wow...March has certainly been an eye-opening month. I think I finally realized what I always knew in my heart: im not someone who really cares deeply about research/public policy and therefore im not really someone that belongs in top-ranked research schools. Im SO HAPPY with UCSD tho...i really have no regrets! I'd say that, for the most part, March has been an ego-check:

U Penn rejection
Columbia rejection
Yale rejection
Michigan waitlist
Baylor waitlist/rejection

and now only harvard needs to reject me....hehe. IVY LEAGUES HATE ME LOL!!!!

Looks like im headed, thankfully, to UCSD next year!!!! Let everyone else freeze half the year....im going to the BEACH, BABY!!!

3/21 HOLD UP!!!!.........HARVARD ACCEPTANCE (NP)!!!!!!!!! This changes EVERYTHING!!!!!!!
