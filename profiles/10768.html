10-21-05: Just when i thought life was crap and i became one the many forgotten people, some higher being throws me a huge juicy bone with this UCSD invite. I was so bored on the laptop then i saw a Gmail notifier pop up. Said 'invitation.' at that point i didn't care from where. Then i saw UCSD. My heart pounded so fast.

01-11-06: Oh i'm so tired of all this. Just got back from U of Wisc and Case Western. Well atleast I got the chance to visit chicago and see my friend.

01-25-06: Life sucks, 4 waitlists 0 acceptances. Time to really look at what I should be doing next year... reapply? find alternative? Live life as a day trader?(i just started playing stocks, it's exhilarating)

03-13-06: Wow i finally cared enough to update my profile. I've been pretty depressed with all these waitlists. On sunday, my GMAIl and ebay accts were both hacked and i was locked out. Oh yea, i've been recieving waves of rejection letters from all sorts of schools and for the remaining schools that haven't contacted me about an interview, i'm treating htem as rejections.

Univ of Wisconsin will ROCK. I love the 'houses' like in harry potter. And the Health science building is magnificient although the hospital needs a freakin MAP. I'll dedicate my year 1 to creating a comprehensive map of that maze.

05-16-2006 Case Western Reserves University called and offered me a spot. We'll see how the financial aid package compare before I make my decision.
