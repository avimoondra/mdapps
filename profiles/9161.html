Summary of Application Experience

Man, this is expensive so far.

All right, so I think I applied later than I should have (most of my apps were completed in october); however, I still think that I should have seen more interviews. A couple former adcom members agreed. It seems like a good chunk of schools have my file pending re-review before even extending an interview, so I am not too hopeful. I'm counting on Case to come through, but I'm starting to come to the realization that I might be reapplying. Unfortunately, there is no short term fix to my situation because the only thing really lacking is my gpa. I'm TAing for anatomy again and volunteering at a homeless clinic while maintaining my research position to help keep me busy.

Other than my gpa, my app was pretty solid. I think that I might have had one lukewarm letter of rec, but that's it, and my personal statement was pretty good. Oh well, back to the daily grind.

AMCAS Submitted 7/2

AMCAS Certified 8/15

4/20/07-Albany accepted me despite a very unpleasant faculty interview.

7/18/07-Didn't make it into Case, so I will be attending Albany in the fall. The application process is officially over. Yay!!
