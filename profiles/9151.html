Summary of Application Experience

7-13-06 Just sent my AMCAS today. Hope it doesn't take too long for verification.

8/4/06 AMCAS finally verified. that took a while.


9/7/06 Have sent 13 secondaries back so far. Ohio state was a really tough one, glad to get it done! Am having mini freak out right now because my last recommender said he hand delivered my letter yeserday and the office says that it is nowhere to be found!!! meanwhile the deadline looms for EVMS (9/14) and VCU (9/22) I just called recommender and left message - hopefully he will call back asap. I need to put in the letter request by tomorrow if they are going to make it by the 14th! AHHH!!

Called Albany today because I never recieved their secondary and they send it to all applicants. She said I'd get one soon. Hope so, geez!

Also, I called VCU and emailed them about whether stats counts as math but no response! Im putting down stats - can't wait anymore.

It seems to me that everything in this applicaiton process has to be a nightmare.

9-13-06 Got Albany secondary the day after my last post. Which is good except for the fact that the essay is 'describe yourself'. Ay yi yi. Letters are finally done with, they realllly held up my app for a while, I hope I am not too late to get some interviews. GW and creighton emailed me notfication that I am complete there. Good stuff. Finished Tufts last night. Had to cut down my essays a LOT which was painful. Tufts is one of my top choices though so it was worth it. Hoping to finish all these by end of september. I need to see a light at the end of the tunnel.

9-24-06
Had a ridiculous day yesterday. It begins on friday as I was messing around online at work and decided on a whim to see what questions there were for Davis. So I look on there and on a whim decide to read the five paragraph essay they call 'directions'. Im reading and notice it says 'within 30 days of secondary request'. For about two hours I just did other things, went to lunch.. and all of a sudden it hit me that the secondary was due THAT DAY! And there are 9 essays! So I freaked out and left work in a frenzied rush and when I got home called up Davis to ask about their policy on this and a very bitchy self-important lady picks up the phone and tells me that a couple days late will not matter and they arent going to withdraw my app. SO that was good to hear.
You might think the Davis saga is over.. but NO! Its last night (saturday) and I am finished but am checking over my work and making small changes like I always do. There are two parts to this monster secodnary and I pressed submit for what I thought was Part I. But instead it submitted the whole thing! And I hadnt checked over part II which I had made plenty of changes on! So I freaked out. I pressed the back button I tried to resubmit .. I tried it all. It just keeps saying error and my status page says 'Secondary complete 9/23' So Im fucked. Im crying, so upset that after all that ass busting for two days that this happens. SO I clean my room. Then I clean the kitchen. Then I do laundry. Very quickly and obsessively so as not to think about Davis and just lose it. So its 12:30am at this point and I am just playing on the internet and click on the uc davis box. And just on a whim I press the back button and fix the stuff I had wanted to fix. I thought it might be comforting at least to have my work and print it out for future secondaries. Then just for fun I press submit again...and its SUBMITTED!!!!! It was a small miracle! And I didnt think it was real until I logged in to my status page and saw that the date of secondary submission had changed to say 9/24/2006!! Feel rather sure this was some kind of small miracle and that random viewing of Davis secondary on friday, along with random realization two hours later that it was due, along with crazy re-submission story (ive never heard of being able to resubmit after the secondary completion date is set) ..it all makes me feel rather like an interview with them is in the stars. But of course, this is a long shot and I need to not get my hopes up.

In other news, I reallly dont want to go to UCLA. Also, reallly do not want to write cultural competence essay from toledo. I should tell Toledo that Im culturally competent enough to know that some people call the toilet the 'toledo'. Perhaps they should know.

9-25-06
Invited to interview at Drexel! I wish I could get some more Philadelphia interviews so it would cut down on the flight costs.. oh well. Am v. happy!

10-6-06
Drexel interview went pretty well. My interviewer kept me 50 minutes! And there was music playing in the room during the interview.. 'So, what does HMO stand for?' *Dont you wish you girlfriend was hot like me..* It was kind of distracting.

Just called EVMS to check the status of my application and basically got yelled at. I said that I had been complete for maybe a month or so and the lady comes back from hold after like a minute: 'What do you mean youve been complete for months?!' I was like no, thats not what I said. Then she said, 'Oh youve called before, were you complete then or why did you call?' I told her that I called to check that my LORs had been received. Then she says, 'Well there is a huge influx of out of state applications and its not moving at all right now.' All this after it took them like 10 mintues just to find my file. What a nightmare.

Tried to call Rosalind Franklin but the status check option says: 'I am sorry I am unable to connect you to that destination' Argh!


10-10-06 Oh..the waiting game. Only one more secondary..OHSU. Can't quite believe I'm really almost finished. If I don't get in this year...TOO BAD. I simply could not go through this again.

10-11-06 I called Rosalind Franklin today and they said that I passed the primary screening and am now in a pool of applicants who may be scheduled for an interview. I think this is good! =)

10-13-06 Friday the 13th, and I got my second interview today! Am a little annoyed that its in PA again as I will have to fly out there and spend lots more money. Would have been nice to consolidate and do drexel and penn state at the same time. Oh well, since its rolling admissions its better than I went to Drexel earlier. Sigh. I wonder if I will hear back from them on Oct. 15th? (that is the earliest you can be accepted if not early decision program) Well I have a long time to prepare for the interview, its not until end of November. But anyway, yay!

11-10-06 Got back from Albany yesterday - it went well! I prepared A LOT for the ethics questions and I am so glad I did because if I hadn't it would have been realllly stressful. There were 7 other interviewees and a few seemed pretty flustered after their first interview. Then I got to go see the anatomy lab which was fun. The school is small but the students seemed really cool and like they liked their school a lot. I had two interviews, one with this really nice guy who was totally chill and the other with this older surgeon guy who was kind of testing me, but I was ready for it so it wasnt so bad. I hear albany gets back to people in just a week or two so I hope to hear soon!

Got an invitation by snail mail today to interview at Unversity of Toledo, formerly known at Medical College of Ohio. So excited! Going in Feb. =) Yay!

11-20-06 Got accepted to Drexel! Woo Hoo!!!! I can't believe it. My mom put it best: 'There isnt a sigh of relief big enough for this moment.' wow!

11-29-06 Had interview at Penn State last week and it was v. interesting. The school has great facilities and a friendly vibe. Its supposed to be 6-8 weeks before I hear back. Wait wait!!

12-6-06 Got invitation to interview at UC Davis today! Most exciting invitation so far! WOO HOO!!!! California LOVE

1/3/07 Just got another interview at VCU! I am quite surprised actually and also really glad because my family is from va. Am now looking into doing Toledo and vcu in one trip, and of course I just bought my plane tickets last night and so if I do them in one trip will have to pay some kind of expensive fee to change the ticket. Sigh. However, its hard to get upset about stuff like this because hey, This is Medical School Admissions - its supposed to try your patience, make you cry in happiness and scream of frustration. And once youve been down that road, airplane ticket fees are small potatoes.

1/10/07 Had interview at UToledo and really loved it. Great facilities and friendly environment. My interviewers were both nice and supportive, had read my application (this is actually a big plus cuz they usually dont) and had encouraging things to say. I need to run to Vromans and pick up some thank you notes. I stayed in the Hilton next to the school, the first time I've ever stayed in a Hilton and boy was it awesome. I went in the hot tub twice! And in the morning I got room service.. cereal brought to my door! I actually ended up forgetting to bring a hair brush with me and spent a lot of time in the morning trying to make it look like I hadn't forgotten my hairbrush. But it took a while and I ended up running out the door like a crazy person in my suit and high heels down the Hilton corridor. Then I walk outside and consider whether or not to walk or drive to the school since I only have 15 min before I am supposed to be there.. I decide to drive and turns out there is frost on my windshield! I didnt know what to do! I tried the wipers but they just scratched over it so i sat up really tall and just looked over the frost and managed to get there. It was slightly ridiculous, but I did end up getting there about three minutes early.. last one there of course. The guy who talked to us was nice, I was having a little trouble staying up though because this fool called me the night before at 12:30am and woke me up.. after I had tried SO hard to get myself to sleep! But thankfully my interviews weren't right away in the morning. Lovely place and I should be hearing in about 6-8 weeks I think. =) *HOPE*

1/13/07- Got a letter from penn state via snail mail teling me that I am on 'hold' and will hear after may 15th. Kinda bummed.

1/16/07- Got invitation today to interview at EVMS! I am so surprised! They were pretty mean to me over the phone when I was checking my status like three months ago. But whatever..I'm excited to go!

Also called Albany today and after four tries got someone on the line. I am still 'under consideration'.. basically I'm on the waitlist and I have to wait to hear until later on. The lady was very nice and assured me this was a 'good thing' which I silently disagreed with. Oh well, big surprise its...MORE WAITING!

1/18/07 - So I am sitting at Starbucks yesterday reading and waiting for it to be time to go to my student's house when my blackberry vibrates.. I look and its from SLU requresting an interview! So I call my mom and tell her. Its time for me to go at this point so I get to my student's house and ask her mom if its okay if I use the internet to schedule my interview, because they are offering 4 interview days in Los Angeles and I really really want to get one before the slots fill up. (save me so much time and $ than flying out there to Missouri!) So I get on the computer and turns out I need a password. So I try going to gmail. It doesnt work. Their computer is dial up so I think, eh, maybe its just slow. So I call my mom and ask her to log into my gmail. And guess what: Gmail is DOWN! Gmail for fucks sake! Its never been down before and all of a sudden in this tiny window that I desperately need gmail, it doesnt work! I nearly had a heart attack, pacing around the room at my kid's house as she looks on at crazy tutor having a freak out attack and shaking my hands and fingers very rapidly as I am wont to do when nervous. Then I try using my blackberry to get my password from gmail and this turns out to work for some reason. (why would gmail work on blackberry and not computer?) So I log on and of course the interview days on Saturday and Sunday have been taken up (all in the last 30 min. - crazy pre-meds), but there was still Monday and Tuesday left. So I scheduled it and managed to keep it in LA..but wow was it nervewracking!

Then early this morning, and by early I mean ass crack of dawn around 6:17 am, I hear the phone ring and a message is left by the super bitchy lady from EVMS. I get up in a half asleep stupor and call her back:

lady: An interview spot just opened up for Jan XX
me: um. that day actually doesn't work for me. but thank you for keeping me -
*dial tone*

I mean really, shes just sooo busy she can't wait for me to say '..in mind' and then say 'bye' like a normal human being using the telephone? Honestly.

med school is turning me into a sleep deprived crazy. though surely this is only the tip of the iceberg.

1/27/07 - Got accepted to U of Toledo today! I can't believe I heard so soon! Awesome..=)

3/12/07 - Debating whether to send an LOI (Intent/interest) to Davis. I am still undecided about davis vs. toledo. Which may very well be crazy, but there it is. How to write an LOI that hits the right tone (ie - I love you and hope you love me rather than I love you and if you dont love me I might kill myself in a fit of pre-med passion) is going to be tough.

My faculty interviewer at Davis told me I am 'on the cusp' and that there are 'a lot applicants like you.' This was so pleasant to hear during an interview, but shows me that I may very well need an LOI to push me over the edge of the 'cusp' or whatever. Davis' second look weekend is April 13-14th and I'd really like to have a decision by then so I can go (or not) and get a feel for the school again which will help me make my decision. And I realllly need help making a decision, so it'd be very valuable.

Been rejected from lots of schools so far and its really okay with me. The only school I think I'd be willing to go another interview for is Loyola Strich. I read on SDN that they are sending invites through march and interviewing through April, so I am holding out a small candle of hope for them. But otherwise, its looking like either Davis or Toledo, one of which I didn't even know the name of OR where it was (what state? where?) when I applied and the other which I thought I had zero chance of ever even receiving an interview at.

March 15th is when VCU will be doing their status updates and I am interested to see if I am accepted/rejected/WL. I think the school is too expensive, but I'm interested to find out nonetheless.

3/16/07 So march 15th came and went, no status update. The updates in the past have all been around 6:00-6:05 EST So everyone on SDN is counting down the minutes, and the seconds.. and then NOTHING. There were two people who were accepted and posted, one in state and one out of state. But everyone else was left high and dry. My comment: Thank you medical school admissions process for yet another fun afternoon. Schools make us wait for MONTHS, then give us a date and don't deliver? Its mean. Anyway someone called them today and apparently they only sent out acceptances yesterday. So all the waitlists and rejections have yet to be notified. Guess that means I'm either waitlisted or rejected. Word on the street however is that the out of state waitlist moves pretty well.

Oh! And I called Loyola yesterday (you are only allowed to call on Thursdays - rolls eyes) and they told me, again, that my file had been flagged for further review. Considering sending them an LOI. I dunno I dunno I dunno!! Also still thinking about Davis LOI. *pressure pressure*

4-17-07 I can't quite believe it, but I got accepted to Davis!!! I called the office and told them my name.. then she looked it up and didn't say anything for a mintue so I was afraid she was figuring out a nice way to tell me I'd been rejected. When she said I had been accepted I started crying and hyperventilating and the lady on the phone said that I was going to make her cry too haha. Anyway turns out they made a decision on my application on April 13th. The day before the second look weekend, which I would realllly have liked to go to. Oh, twisted fate.
I talked to bearness this morning and he got a job offer this morning in AZ! So I declared today a special day for us - and we're def going to celebrate when he gets back. Yay April 17th!

5-24-07 Got my last rejection letter today in the mail, from NYMC. Cry me a river.. not! But it marks the official end of this crazy application process.. what a ride! Good luck to the '08 class, I hope this profile is helpful! Adieu=)
