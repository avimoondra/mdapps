Brief Profile:
I am creating this profile to motivate others who may have had similar experiences as me (overcame obstacles such as language and cultural barriers, grew up in poverty). My MCAT score was below par unfortunately and I didn't have time to retake, but I still managed to get into medical school by making the other parts of my application as strong as possible. I just want to say that there is hope for anyone who has what it takes to become a doctor. Don't let any one thing bring you down!
