Summary of Application Experience

i loved mayo so much and have been offered the dean's scholarship!

withdrew from most my schools after acceptance at mayo (don't really think i could choose another school over mayo and the scholarship anyways) except for the ones close by - in case it is necessary for me to be close to home next year... (and duke because i am curious and think i would regret not at least visiting)

1/25 got an invite for harvard new pathway--thought the east coast was dead to me...i donno how interested i am in harvard but its still exciting to have gotten some looove...i am going to make this profile public so that people in my similar situation can look at this some day and have some encouragement...i had the most pre-med post-bac angst ever trying to apply with a sub 3.5 gpa. and got a lot of flak from my family for taking time off after graduating and not getting a 'real job' (read: make some monayys) in order to pursue my dream (med school). mayo acceptance and the opportunity to interview at places (at all!!) has been an answered prayer and blessing as i def dont deserve this based on my stats O_o!!!

o yea..and i was also a 'later applicant' (amcas verified 8/20) and apps to some places that i had/have interviews for were sent ON the deadline (harvard, duke, stanford, iowa) - i'm not yet even complete at OHSU and JHU to get an idea of exactly HOW late i am at some schools...so was obviously even more angsty...anyways it can happen ('it' being acceptance at a top choice (that is out of my league!!) and interviews at places that are out of my league)--stay humble, hopeful, happy, helpful (haha my lame 4H's of how im trying to handle this process)

2/7 had my reg ucla program interview yesterday...the day was kinda meHhh .intvw during the tour so i dint get to see anything before i had to rush back home to celebrate chinese new year...

4/4/08 interviewed at ucla-prime this past weekend and thought it was awesome =). LA was awesome a lot more fun than i expected


4/11/08 was accepted to ucla-prime-westwood (one of my top choices!!) on 4/9 and so withdrew from davis rural-prime today as the waiting was just too angsty! cant wait for ucla revisit next weekend =D still waiting on hopkins and finaid packages before i can make any final decisions O_o;

4/21/08 o man..ucla visit was awesome...really really happy and excited about prime!!! am hoping some scholarships will pull through

5/7/08 --umm weird got accepted to davis post-withdrawal...haha i emailed teri twice to withdraw...second second look (they have two this year i guess) is this weekend and it looks fun...but such an awk situation....

revisited mayo...glad i revisited...i think i would have been VERy disappointed if i had attended there based off my impressions as a naive interviewee...still their scholarships/finaid far exceeds what the UC's can offer me...so holdin on...hopefully gloria at ucla will get back to me -___-!!!
