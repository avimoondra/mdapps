Summary of Application Experience

tadaaaa. i finally filled one of these out. if nothing else just for myself.

playing basketball for my school really limited the type and amount of activities in which i could get involved. my coach will change practice times when she wants to with little warning. i didn't think it was fair to commit to something and then always have to call and cancel because of practice. i tried to volunteer at a hospital this past school year (i finally got a car), but then i hurt myself playing basketball and had to be on crutches for 4 months. it was too hard for me to help with anything, so i decided not to volunteer at the hospital. i go to a very small school (~1400 undergrad), and we have only 5 full time bio profs and 3 full time chem profs. plus, only 3 bio and 1 chem prof do research. this made it very difficult to get involved in research. add in basketball and a lack of a car/4 months of crutches and it is practically impossible.

so here is my process:
-Early June: sent in transcript to AMCAS
-12 June: AMCAS sends me an email saying they got my transcript...yay!! (my school isn't so smart at sending stuff in sometimes)
-15 June: PS is done, given to various people for editting and comments
-19 June: booooooo to mcat scores, i def. thought i would get double digits in the sciences and a 9 in VR, oh well, rolling with what i have for now
-20 June: submitted AMCAS, marked 'no' on retake cuz i am unsure still
-30 June: received email from AMCAS saying i was verified, received some secondaries (unscreened of course) already, filling them out
-5 July: received a few more secondaries, i should be sending in about 5 before the end of the week, wooohooooo
-6/7 July: received UWASH secondary, already sent it in, yesssss
-16 July: turned some more in, trying not to stress, still waiting for one more letter!!!!
-18 July: things are really slow on the secondary front
-20 July: added a few more kinda randomly, learned Wake doesn't want me, meh, whatever
-21 July: gosh darn letters, hurrrrrryyyyyy up, i am so tempted just to send in what i have now, but the letters i am waiting on are going to be dang good grrrrrrrrrrr
-25 July: woohooo, received penn state and loyola, the more i look into penn state, the more i like it, my final letter writer if faxing her letter tomorrow to interfolio, i will hopefully be complete at most of my schools by late next week, wooohoooo
-27 July: just got Upstate, man, that was quick and easy, just send them money, first one like that that i have received, very nice!
-28 July: all my letters were sent off by interfolio today!!!!!! soooo excited
-4 August: wow, august already, i am complete at quite a few schools, in the process of sending 3/4 more apps off this weekend
-8 August: received toledo/ohio/whatever they are calling it now, hoping to send OHSU off by today and Toledo off by tomorrow, we will see
-15 August: yayayay, Drexel's was another easy one, just fill out and send money, i was glad to get Wayne States yesterday, hopefully i can have that sent off by tomorrow-----got EMORY!! last one baby, i have 4 to send still, woohoooo, c'mon interviews :D
-18 August: **sigh** one (maybe two) more to send in, unsure whether i will do tulane or not, i kinda don't want to spend the money i think, at the latest, i will send wayne st out my monday cuz i leave for school EARLY tuesday morning, last day of work today though, yipeeee!!!
-23 August: sent in the last one this morning, I HATE MOVING!!
-28 August: holy crap!!! first day of school and first invite...go hoyas!
-29 August: even holier of crap!! UW interview!!! ahhhhhhh!!!!!! invite came via email........excited
-7 Sept: it's my half birthday!!! interview invite from RFU!! yay
-8 Sept: CREIGHTON!!!!! wow, i am going to have 4 fridays, 4 interviews, crazy
-13 Sept: finally got the guts to call some schools to work out some problems, wasn't as hard as i thought it would be
-17 Sept: received my VCU update, i hear it isn't a bad one, so that is good, now for more waiting :(
-18 Sept: pre-interview hold at Upstate, we will see how this goes, received via smail mail
-30 Sept: exhausted, back from my first interview, went okay i think
-6 Oct: ahhhh, it's october already, back from my second interview, i've like both schools thus far, i'm tired
-9 Nov: wow, it's been a while.......accepted!!!!! yessss, i can finally relax some
-24 Nov: i am in 'continue to review' pools for both gtown and UW. still waiting to hear from RFU. just spent turkey day away from my family. just lost a basketball game, and i am still kinda injured. not in the greatest of moods. i just need to remind myself of how lucky i am that i received an early acceptance.
-11 Dec: wow, its december.....received rejection letter from albany today. it's amazing how these things just roll off once you've been accepted
-22 Jan: i guess i am really lazy, i have sent updated grades to Loyola and UW, will try to get an LOI off to UW sometime this week, still unsure about whether or not i will go to the VCU interview, if anything, i will reschedule it for after basketball season because the date i have right now falls just before our last home games, hmmmm
-30 Jan: basketball is going really really really well....yay! nothing really new on the med school front
-5 Feb: just got a rejection email from Colorado, meh, didn't think i had much of a chance anyway, i have decided not to go to the VCU interview, it is just too in the way of basketball. i think i broke my pinky as well. sad day.
-12 March: just turned 22, spent spring break in North Dakota finishing up my college basketball career, returned home to find 4 rejections, oh well
-9 July: holy crap, this has all gone so fast. i am more than lucky to have an acceptance, but am scared sh!tless to be starting in about 5 weeks. so glad i didn't retake the MCAT, i did just fine with what i had. apply early and to a ton of schools (if you are in doubt of your chances)!!!! so what it costs a little more money, better now than having to do it all again. i got rejected by my states school in march, which sucks because my BF and i are both living in WA right now. we are trying to figure out what the future holds for the two of us. moving down to omaha in about 4 weeks. good luck to everyone out there!!
