07/04/2007-The schools listed above are all schools that I'm considering applying to but have not as of yet. I think I will need to cut some schools just because I don't have enough money to travel to such far distances.

Among other concerns, one of my largest is that I earned an F in Calc II 3rd semester. I did retake the class, and got a B. Although my GPA is low, only looking at my last two years of undergrad, I have 16 As, 3 B+s, and 1 B. My BCPM during those last two years is 3.80.

I'm not picky about where I would like to go. I would really love the opportunity to go to any US med school, and at this point, the bigger issues are: 'Which schools will accept me?' and 'To how many schools can I afford to apply?'
