2/17: There go my California dreams... whatever
3/23: Wow, I'm a pro at getting waitlisted... now I'm sure there's a rejection or waitlist letter at home waiting for me. Come on, Yale, you're my only hope!

3/31: Rejection - my only one post-interview. I guess they didn't like what they saw.


A note for future applicants - be enthusiastic about the school at which you're interviewing. After my Umich interview and acceptance, I didn't give a hoot about the other schools and it showed. I think that's why I got the results I did. Oh well, I'm really happy at Umich.
