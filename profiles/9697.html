Summary of Application Experience

Senior spring: Thesis due March 1, MCAT April 16, History and Literature Oral Exam May 17. That was fun. But it turned out well.

So, took the April '05 MCAT, filled out most of last year's AMCAS (except PS... aargh), then got offered my two-year commitment job in August, so waited one more year. Now I'm in a chorus in Houston and taking (introductory) Spanish classes on the weekends. Definitely ready to get started!

Update 6/23/06: Turned in AMCAS and TMDSAS 6/20/06, waiting to be verified. Took off San Antonio since I never took Biochem. Taking a bio course at HBU since I only had 12/14 required bio credits for TX public schools.

Update 6/30/06: AMCAS was verified yesterday, though there was a small mistake. I decided to have them fix it, which seems not to require going back to 'Ready for Review' but rather stays 'Complete,' so my application should still be going out to schools. Eeee! TMDSAS got my check/pictures/certification page 6/28/06, so hopefully they should send things out soon.

Update 7/13/06: TMDSAS _finally_ processed my application - there was a quick visit to the 'Problem Section' a couple days ago due to some confusion over residency status - everything's cleared up now. Bio class ended on Tuesday, finally, so my transcript from HBU should be on its way to TMDSAS soon. Now for secondaries. Ugh.

Update 8/28/06: Not doing too well with the secondaries - finally sent in NYU (no essays!) and Baylor ('special experiences' - argh!), I think I'm almost done with Cornell, and I have to write some stuff about what kind of career I want for UTSW and Stanford, plus come up with how my particular diversity influenced my goals and prep for my career (Stanford... who else?). Sigh. LORs should be forthcoming soon, at which point I will have no excuses left as to why these essays aren't done...

Update 9/26/06: FINALLY getting 'Complete's! TMDSAS received my letters yesterday and Baylor, Cornell and NYU all tell me I'm complete today. Plus, got an interview invite to UTSW on 9/23 for 10/14!

Update 1/17/07: It's been awhile... so apparently I only get interviews in even months (August: UTH and UTMB, October: UTSW, December: Emory, February: Baylor.) Bad news on the Cornell site today - you never want to hear about a pre-interview admissions decision! Too bad, I loved Cornell's philosophy and I love NYC... maybe residency. NYU has just been silent, and at this point no news is definitely bad news, since I believe they're done interviewing. But I am so super-excited about Baylor! It's been my first choice for ages, and I would be sososo happy to go there.

Update 2/15/07: Match this morning, matched to UT-H, rumors are that UTSW didn't participate. Waiting... especially for Baylor!

Update 7/3/07: Waitlisted at Baylor in April. Withdrew from UTSW waitlist in May after putting money into buying a condo. Due to having another TX acceptance on June 15, I believe I was removed from the Baylor waitlist and thus will be attending UT-Houston in August!
