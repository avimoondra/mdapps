For future applicants who may be reading this whole, long-winded profile, I say this:

1.) really do get your materials (and everyone else's) in early. Ask for LORs WELL in advance, and keep on top of it.

2.) Don't get your heart set on one school. It's difficult (I know) but this process is such a total crap shoot. While you can be pretty certain (normally) of getting into a certain range/tier/type of school, there is no knowing exactly which school will accept you.

3.) As always, wear comfortable shoes. ;)

Good Luck!
