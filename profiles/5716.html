Summary of Application Experience

When I say complete, I mean I've gotten confirmation from the school that I'm complete.

June 27, 2008: Submitted to AMCAS, waiting verification (Transcripts were received a few weeks prior)

July 18, 2008: AMCAS verified. Bring on the secondaries!

August 4, 2008: 15 secondaries submitted, 15 to go...(theoretically)

August 7, 2008: 20 secondaries submitted, 10 to go...

August 28, 2008: First rejection - Boston U pre-interview, :(

September 3, 2008: I got my first interview invite!!! YAY!!! :D NYMC - interview on Oct 7th! I'm super excited right now!!

September 5, 2008: Second interview: USUHS. They told me the interview would be Oct 2, but I'm asking to change to Oct 9, so I can head there after my NYMC interview.

September 13, 2008: Interview invite to USC. First Cali school to invite me to interview! :D

September 25, 2008: Interview invite to UC Irvine! I'm so excited I'm hearing from Cali schools this early! :D

October 22, 2008: I really really liked USC!! I'll have to see how my other interviews go, but right now USC is definitely my top choice :) They said I'd hear back before Thanksgiving *crosses fingers* Thanksgiving is my favorite holiday, so hopefully they don't ruin it... ;)

October 30, 2008: My first acceptance! USUHS! :D No matter what happens with other schools, I'm going to be a physician! YAY!

December 10, 2008: Got a letter in the mail from USC - Accepted! YAY!!!!! :D :D :D I also got one from NYMC - waitlisted; oh well, it was at the bottom of my list anyway ;)

December 19, 2008: Accepted to Temple!!! :) I really like both USC and Temple so I have no idea how I'm going to make this decision! :/

May 7, 2009: Well, finally, I have decided: USUHS here I come! :) I just felt like it was the best environment for me. I fit in with the students better than at the other schools. All three schools are very good and I would have an excellent education at any of them, so I had to go with my gut. :) Oh, and I'm going into the Navy, if anyone was wondering.
