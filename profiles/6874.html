Summary of Application Experience

AMCAS Submitted 6/21 (Really was done with everything by 6/10 except for the PS. Shouldn't have waited so long to start. START YOUR PS EARLY!!!! Thank you SDN editors you guys were amazing!!!)

AMCAS Verified 7/9 (Took a while...expected that since I submitted with the bulk of the crowd)

6/25 First wave of secondaries! I know they're just automatic ones, but still quite exciting. Got Columbia and GWU.

7/5 Yes! My non-science prof finally sent in her LOR. Was worrying about this woman since she's all the way in England.

7/11 Second wave of secondaries right after verification.

7/12 My PI sent in her LOR. Now I have my two science and one non-science. Should I wait for my other two? I still need to get one from a prof that I TA-ed for and one from my extracurricular.

7/14 Some more. Got Dartmouth and Jefferson. Got an email from UCSD letting me know that they got my AMCAS. Got me excited for no reason. Got a similar one from Washington in St Louis. Damn...I wish those were secondaries.

7/17 More. I got UCI, Brown, and Albert Einstein. Does UCI screen? My first California school!!! I'm super excited! I decided not to wait for my other two LOR's. I'll just have to spend some more money and send those in later.

7/21 Damit my last two LOR writers are still lagging it. I got more secondaries pouring in. I used to be so excited about seeing secondary invitations in my inbox. Now I just dread it.

8/11 Got second MCAT score. So nervous the night before. But good news. I went from 31 => 34. I dropped one point in my biological sciences though. I went from 12 => 11. Kinda irritated that I kept second guessing myself, but hey I'm not complaining, score's higher.

12/30 Found out I was in the bigger pile at UCSD. Wrote out a plea for a secondary. They actually granted it! And even more insane, I got an offer for interview in Feb! INSANE. These appeal letters really do work. Going to interview at NYMC next week. Still waiting on more schools.
