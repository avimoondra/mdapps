Summary of Application Experience

6/20 - Submitted primary application.
6/30 - Primary app verified.
7/3 - Added BU, Tufts, and GW.
8/29 - First interview invite! :)
9/12 - First rejection. Oh well, it had to happen sometime.
9/17 - Received a vague VCU status update: 'The committee's file review indicates that they are interested in your candidacy. However, due to the increase in the applicant pool this year, it is clear we MAY be unable to interview all the candidates at your priority score. If we do find that we are able to grant an interview you will be notified by e-mail.' So, who knows.
9/18 - Had a mock interview today, went really well. Was told I have good posture and a nice smile, and to have more self-confidence in my answers. At least I now feel like slightly less of a clown wearing my suit.
10/16 - I got the most amazing phone call of my life! Accepted to NJMS!!!
10/18 - I am withdrawing most of my applications. I'm pretty sure NJMS is where I want to go, and there's no reason to keep my application in at other places where I don't want to go and other applicants do.
