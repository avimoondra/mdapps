Summary of Application Experience



9/4 update: Still have 5 more secondaries to complete. Hopefully, will be able to submit another one later today and be finish with the rest by the end of this week. I doubt that I'll submit the G'town one. The fee is kinda ridiculous in my opinion.

9/12 update: So happy with the invites I've gotten so far.

9/19 update: I am still working on 4 more secondaries. This process is so long and ridiculous. Luckily, we have 6-7 more months of uncertainty to endure *sarcasm.* I didn't apply to BU, because I would rather remain in NYC if I don't get into one of my top (re: reach schools).

December update: I'm glad with the way things are going right now. I was accepted by Jefferson first, but I will probably turn them down. The people there were nice, but very homogeneous and I wasn't sure if I would fit in too well. Also, on my interview day, I barely saw any URM students and I was not too comfortable with that. The Cornell acceptance was great! I really fell in love with the school during my day there. Very nice people, great facilities, great location! About SUNY Downstate, I loved the overall working class/first generation American vibe that I got from there. It really appealed to me and a lot of the students I met were 'firsts (i.e. first to go to med school, first to pursue professional school, first to graduate college, etc.) in their fam.

While I'm heavily leaning to Cornell, I'm holding onto the acceptances for a little bit until I see finaid packages.
