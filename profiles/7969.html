Summary of Application Experience

6/??/07 - I submitted AMCAS and was completed less than a week later (transcript sent in late May)

8/29/07 - All of my received secondaries are submitted and I'm complete at 22 places!

9/13/07 - I just realized how many schools 25 really is. Thus far I'm extremely pleased with my progress and just wish scheduling interviews wasn't so hard. For those applying in upcoming years, plan to miss A TON of school (and schedule your classes accordingly).

9/14/07 - Invited for Case Western - need to call their MSTP office to schedule my interview, hopefully they have the 11th and 12th of October open!

9/17/07 - First interview for Case is 10/17-19. This unfortunately conflicts with AECOM. Because I haven't confirmed AECOM, I'm going to try and reschedule (but for when???). I really really want a Hopkins interview on 10/11 but it probably won't happen - such is life.

9/20/07 - Northwestern interview! Must reschedule all the way back to December because of other interview conflicts - this is going to be a busy busy semester! Penn interview! WOW. sucks that I have to reschedule...I'm really excited to visit Penn - powerhouse for cancer research and translational research!

9/21/07 - Anyone who says interview invites DON'T come in floods is just plain wrong - got an invite from UTSW today! Super-psyched! Hopefully I can go to the State Fair of Texas while I'm in Dallas - should be interesting. So, for those of you looking at my calendar: yes, I'll be gone every weekend for about 6 weeks, one week off (thus far) in November then another interview immediately before Thanksgiving. I really hope I get an acceptance soon so I can start to 'triage' interview invites etc. - I swear I'll start withdrawing once I have a single acceptance!

9/24/07 - Lots of back and forth communication with Northwestern and AECOM - finally have those interviews scheduled. I feel bad for scheduling them so late after the invite but I guess that's how it goes...

9/27/07 - Just got to my hotel at Vanderbilt and checked my email. Interview at Mount Sinai! AND what's even better is that it coincides perfectly with my Cornell interview which means one ticket will suffice (woohoo for saving $$$!)....I figure I'll go to Cornell's Thursday, Friday, and part of Saturday, hang out with my friend who has an apartment in NYC on Saturday, Sunday and part of Monday and then do Mount Sinai stuff Monday and Tuesday then fly home for Thanksgiving.

LATER: Loving Vanderbilt - people are nice, weather is awesome, the director is HILARIOUS. Just got UCLA and UCSD interviews!

9/29/07 - Just got back from the Vandy interview. I think it's ruined the rest of my interviews - they treated us really really well. The actual interview day is completely exhausting with 6 separate interviews, 4 of which are allowed to run for an hour (which all of mine did). Afterwards the students took us out to a nice restaurant (everything paid for by the program) and then we went to a couple of bars after dinner. Saturday consisted of a tour (Nashville is awesome!) and brunch at a student's house (really nice place - housing is relatively cheap).

10/1/07 - First rejection(s)! UCSF and Johns Hopkins both sent rejections at the same time, which I received (both) today - OUCH! Oh well, at least that means 2 fewer interviews to schedule!

10/8/07 - Back from WashU...words to describe the experience: INTENSE, EXHAUSTING, IMPRESSIVE, AWESOME

10/11/07 - Columbia invite! Must reschedule though because I don't think I can get from San Diego to NYC by the time the day starts...

10/12 - UTSW interviews went well - pretty laid back compared to Vanderbilt and WashU. I was waiting all day for a phone call from WashU saying I was rejected. When we were there they said they only call people who are rejected so I figured by 5:30pm and no phone call, I was safe - WRONG. I checked my email after interviews were over and saw that I got rejected...kinda lame to get my hopes up just to stomp on them via email. Oh well, obviously I didn't do nearly as well as I thought and they just didn't like me. On the upside, many of my future interviews are at schools with stronger cancer research...

10/17/07 - Got a call from my favorite director Terry Dermody telling me I was accepted to the Vanderbilt MSTP!!! Also - Harvard invite!

10/19/07 - Waiting in the Cleveland airport for > 3 or 4 hours is kinda lame. On the upside Case was fun but exhausting. I definitely prefer having 7 interviews in one day and being done instead of having 7 interviews over the span of 2 days. Got a call from Dennis Mckearin today inviting me back to UTSW for their second round of interviews...fun times ahead!

11/14/07 - Got an email from Ron Koenig - accepted to Michigan MSTP!!! It all of the sudden hit me that I'm going to have to make a decision about where I spend the next 8 years - scary!

11/18/07 - Haven't really updated in a while but long story short - I absolutely LOVE CORNELL!!! NYC isn't too bad either. When you combine that with the amazing research at Weill, Sloan-Kettering, and Rockefeller, it's a hard combo to beat - I just hope I get in!

11/19/07 - Got a call from a 216 area code during class. The voicemail said to call the office back - kinda scary! Anyways, I called Dr. Ruth Keri back and she said I was accepted at Case Western!!! All of the sudden I realize I'm going to have to make a decision about where I end up - it won't be decided for me and that just seems scary...hopefully over winter break I'll have time to really sit down and think about these places and rank them and get a better feel during second looks - exciting but scary!

12/15/07 - Just got back from Penn and OMG I love everything about the program - its size, the location (Philly!!!), the funding, the research, and even better - THE PEOPLE....sigh I just hope I don't get crushed like I did with Cornell. So far no word from Cornell meaning either rejection or waitlist - I'm optimistic that it's a waitlist since some people have received rejections post-interview but you never know....I'm pretty sure at this point Penn is my top choice and I'll just have to figure out when to send an LOI - and hopefully they liked me half as much as I liked them - no more interviews 'til 2k8!!!

Just got my letter of rejection from Cornell - boo for them....

3/12/08 Just withdrew from 4 schools...will fill in the gaps later

3/27/08 ACCEPTED TO PENN!!!! Penn quickly became my top choice after visiting in December. I'm so excited to be a part of their program! Philly here I come!
